# Cuadros de Mando Power BI Review 2026: Is This Free Skool Community Worth Joining?

Cuadros de Mando Power BI is a free, Spanish-language Skool community with 569 members centered on a single tool and a single promise: build Power BI dashboards with KPIs that genuinely improve your decisions, framed so that both the individual member and their company benefit from the growth.

## What's Actually Inside

Unlike communities that promise generic "data skills" or "business intelligence" training, this one names its tool directly, Power BI, which gives a much clearer sense of what members are actually building. The emphasis on KPIs that "really improve decisions" implies the content goes beyond dashboard aesthetics into choosing metrics that matter, rather than just visualizing whatever data happens to be available. The "you grow, your company grows" framing suggests the audience is likely analysts or operators building reporting for their own employer rather than freelance BI consultants building for clients.

## Pros

- Free to join, so there's no cost barrier to seeing what the community actually covers.
- Specific tool focus (Power BI, not BI tools in general) sets clearer expectations than vaguer "learn data" communities.
- Framing around decision-useful KPIs, rather than just dashboard design, suggests an emphasis on business impact over visuals alone.

## Cons

- The description doesn't indicate skill level, whether the content assumes beginner Power BI knowledge or dives into more advanced DAX and Power Query work.
- No independent reviews of this community were found beyond its own Skool listing.

## Who It's a Good Fit For

Spanish-speaking analysts, operators, or business owners who already use or plan to use Power BI and want their dashboards tied more directly to decisions rather than just reporting for its own sake.

## Who Should Skip It

People who use a different BI tool, such as Tableau or Looker, or anyone looking for English-language instruction, since the tool and language focus here are both specific.
