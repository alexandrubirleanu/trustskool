## What's Actually Inside

Three specific mechanisms are named as the backbone of the system: sales funnels, paid advertising, and AI tools, all aimed at automating the parts of MLM recruiting that are traditionally manual, cold messaging contacts, chasing warm-market leads, booking calls one by one. Calling the system "Ma Machine," my machine, in the name itself reinforces that positioning: this is presented as a repeatable engine to plug into an existing MLM business, not the business opportunity itself. The description doesn't name which MLM company or companies the system is designed for, nor which specific ad platforms or funnel tools are used, so members would need to confirm those specifics apply to their own MLM business before joining.
