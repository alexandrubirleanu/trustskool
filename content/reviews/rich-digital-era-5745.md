## What's Actually Inside

The phrase "secure contracts" is the most telling detail in the description, it suggests the community is oriented toward helping drivers move from app-based, per-delivery gig work toward direct contracts with businesses or logistics companies, which typically means more stable and potentially higher income. The distinction the description draws, building "real income not just run routes," reinforces that the community's value proposition is about business development for drivers, not just tips on driving efficiently.

## Pros

- Open to both aspiring and current drivers, so it can serve people at different stages of a courier career.
