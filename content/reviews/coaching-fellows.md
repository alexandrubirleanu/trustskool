# Coaching Fellows Review 2026: Is This Free German Coaching Community Worth Joining?

Coaching Fellows is a free, 352-member German-language Skool community pitched as "part of a movement", its own description invites members to "be heard together and grow your business in a future-proof, ethical way," and explicitly mentions an included marketing tool and lead system as part of the offer.

## What's Actually Inside

Two things stand out in the description beyond the movement framing: an emphasis on "ethical" growth, suggesting the community positions itself against high-pressure or manipulative sales tactics common in some coaching spaces, and the mention of a concrete marketing tool plus lead system bundled in. That combination points to a community offering both philosophy (how to market ethically) and practical infrastructure (actual tools for generating leads) rather than one without the other.

Bundling a tool with community access is a meaningfully different offer than a pure discussion space, since it implies members get something functional to use immediately rather than only advice to implement on their own, though how robust that tool actually is can't be judged from the description alone.

## Pros

- Free to join, removing cost as a barrier to testing whether the ethical-growth framing matches your own values and business style.
- Explicitly names concrete included assets, a marketing tool and lead system, rather than only vague community access.
- Positions itself around a shared identity ("a movement") which can appeal to coaches looking for peer connection, not just tactics.

## Cons

The description doesn't specify what the marketing tool or lead system actually are, software, templates, scripts, so it's worth confirming details before assuming a specific format. We also found no independent reviews of this community beyond its own Skool listing.

## Who It's a Good Fit For

German-speaking coaches who want to grow their business through what the community frames as ethical marketing, and who want practical lead-generation tools bundled with community support.

## Who Should Skip It

Non-German speakers, and coaches specifically looking for aggressive, high-pressure sales training, would likely find the ethical-growth framing here a mismatch. Coaches who already have their own marketing tools and lead system in place may also see less added value in a bundled tool they don't need.
