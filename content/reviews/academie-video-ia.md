# A.V.I (Académie Vidéo IA) Review 2026: Is $9/Month Worth It for AI Video?

The description frames the goal as impact ("percutants," punchy or impactful) rather than just technical output.

## What's Actually Inside

Two content areas sit at the center of the description: AI image generation and AI video generation, both aimed at practical output formats, advertisements, social posts, and short films. That framing suggests the community targets people creating content for marketing or media purposes rather than hobbyists experimenting with AI art for its own sake. At $9 a month, the price is low enough to function as a mass-market entry point into AI content creation tools rather than a premium production course.

Grouping images and video together under one academy also suggests a workflow-based teaching approach, since many creators now move between still images and short video clips within the same campaign or content series. Someone producing an ad, for instance, might generate a still visual first and then extend it into a short video variant, and a community covering both formats together is better positioned to teach that kind of end-to-end production than one focused on a single medium.

## Pros

- Low $9/month price makes it accessible for creators and small marketers testing AI content tools without a big commitment.
- Clear focus on practical output (ads, posts, films) rather than abstract AI art experimentation.
- Serves French-speaking creators directly, rather than requiring translation of English-language AI tutorials.

## Cons

- The description doesn't name specific AI tools or platforms covered, so members should confirm the toolset matches what they already use or want to learn.
- No independent reviews of this community were found beyond its own Skool listing, and AI video tools evolve quickly, so content currency is worth confirming before joining.

## Who It's a Good Fit For

French-speaking creators, marketers, or small business owners who want to learn to produce AI-generated images and video for advertising and social content at a low monthly cost.

## Who Should Skip It

Anyone looking for training on a single specific AI video tool rather than a general content-creation approach should confirm the curriculum matches first. Non-French speakers won't get full value from a community built around French-language instruction either.
