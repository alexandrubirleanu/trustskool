# Printable Stickers Club Review 2026: Is It Worth $7/Month?

Printable Stickers Club is a 1,616-member Skool community priced at $7/month, built around a simple, recurring deliverable: a bundle of 10 or more printable sticker sheets delivered every month, created by sticker-making specialist Mim Jenkinson according to the community's own description.

## What's Actually Inside

This is a straightforward content-subscription model rather than a coaching or skill-building community: members receive a fresh set of at least 10 printable sticker sheets each month, aimed at people who use planner stickers, journaling stickers, or decorative printables regularly and would otherwise need to buy or design new sheets themselves. The pitch, "never run out of stickers again," positions the value as convenience and steady supply rather than teaching members to design their own stickers from scratch.

## Who Runs It

Mim Jenkinson is named directly in the community's own description as the sticker-making professional behind the monthly bundles. Beyond that attribution in the community's own materials, no further independent background or portfolio verification was available in the public data reviewed.

## Pros

- Extremely low price point ($7/month) relative to buying individual printable sticker packs elsewhere, which often cost more per set.
- A named creator (Mim Jenkinson) attached to the product rather than an anonymous or AI-generated content mill, based on the community's own framing.
- Consistent monthly delivery model gives a predictable, ongoing supply for planner or journaling enthusiasts who use stickers regularly.

## Cons

- No independent reviews of the sticker sheet quality or design variety were found beyond the community's own description.
- As a print-your-own product, value depends on having a decent home printer and cardstock or sticker paper on hand, a cost not included in the $7/month price and not mentioned in the public listing.

## Who It's a Good Fit For

Planner and journaling enthusiasts who regularly use printable stickers and want a low-cost, steady monthly supply of new designs rather than purchasing individual packs elsewhere or hunting down free templates scattered across different websites each month.

## Who Should Skip It

Anyone without access to a printer and sticker paper, or anyone looking to learn sticker design themselves rather than receive ready-made sheets, will find limited value in this specific format. People who only occasionally use stickers may also find a one-off purchase makes more sense than a recurring monthly subscription.
