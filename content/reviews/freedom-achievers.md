# Freedom Achievers Review 2026: Is $49/Month Worth It for Google Ads Dropshipping?

Freedom Achievers is a 344-member Skool community priced at $49 a month, built around a specific and bold claim: reaching $1,000 a day within 90 days through dropshipping powered by Google Ads, explicitly without needing experience, Facebook ads, or TikTok ads. The 90-day timeline gives the pitch a concrete shape, members are meant to see results within a defined window rather than an open-ended "eventually."

## What's Actually Inside

The description positions Google Ads as the core traffic channel, a deliberate contrast to the Facebook and TikTok ad strategies that dominate most dropshipping communities on Skool. This narrower channel focus suggests the curriculum likely covers Google Ads account setup, campaign structuring, and product selection tailored to search and shopping traffic rather than social media ad creative. The "no experience needed" framing signals the content is built for complete beginners rather than assuming existing e-commerce or advertising knowledge, which likely means the earlier lessons cover setup fundamentals, store creation, product research, and basic account structure, before getting into scaling.

## Pros

- A specific, differentiated channel focus, Google Ads, rather than the oversaturated Facebook and TikTok dropshipping playbooks.
- Explicitly framed for beginners with no prior experience, lowering the perceived barrier to entry.
- A clear, quantified target ($1,000/day in 90 days) gives prospective members a concrete benchmark to evaluate against.

## Cons

The $1,000/day in 90 days claim is aggressive, and we found no independent verification of members actually hitting that target, nor independent reviews of this community beyond its Skool listing. At $49/month, prospective members should also budget for Google Ads spend itself on top of the membership fee, since ad costs are a separate, ongoing expense beyond the community fee.

## Who It's a Good Fit For

Aspiring dropshippers specifically interested in Google Ads as a traffic source, who want to avoid the Facebook and TikTok ad landscape and are comfortable treating the $1,000/day claim as an aspirational target rather than a guarantee. People who have already struggled with Facebook or TikTok ad account bans or rejections may also be drawn to a channel less prone to those specific platform issues.

## Who Should Skip It

Anyone with an existing Facebook or TikTok ads dropshipping setup that's already working is unlikely to need to switch channels. People uncomfortable with the extra, variable cost of Google Ads spend on top of the $49/month fee should also factor that into their budget before joining.
