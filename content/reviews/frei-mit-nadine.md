# Auswandern & Online Business Review 2026: Is This Free Community Legit?

Auswandern & Online Business is a free German-language Skool community with 686 members, built around two combined themes: leaving the traditional system behind and building a life abroad, and running an online business to fund that lifestyle. Its description opens with the question "Raus aus dem System?" (out of the system?) and positions itself as an active community for aspiring and current emigrants and online entrepreneurs.

## What's Actually Inside

The pairing of "Auswandern" (emigrating) with "Online Business" is the defining feature here: this isn't purely a relocation-logistics group, nor purely a business-building group, it's specifically for people trying to combine the two, funding a life abroad through location-independent online income. The description promises "echtes Wissen, Feedback & Austausch" (real knowledge, feedback, and exchange), suggesting an active discussion format rather than a static course dump, with members presumably sharing both practical emigration experience and online business tactics.

## Pros

- Free to join, lowering the barrier for people still in the early, exploratory stage of considering a move abroad.
- The combined emigration-plus-online-business angle directly addresses a real and common obstacle: needing income that isn't tied to a physical location before relocating is realistic.
- Explicit emphasis on feedback and exchange suggests an active, conversational community rather than a one-way content feed.

## Cons

- We didn't find independent reviews of this specific community beyond its own Skool listing, so we can't verify how active the exchange actually is in practice.
- The description doesn't specify which countries, visa types, or online business models are the community's focus, so prospective members researching a specific relocation path may need to ask directly before joining.

## Who It's a Good Fit For

German-speaking people actively considering emigration who also want or need an online income stream to support that move are the clearest fit. People earlier in the process, just exploring whether "getting out of the system" is realistic for them, may also find value in a free, low-commitment space to ask questions.

## Who Should Skip It

Non-German speakers will find limited value here, since the community operates in German. People looking purely for emigration logistics without any online business component, or purely for business-building content without any relocation angle, may also find the combined focus isn't a precise match for their needs.
