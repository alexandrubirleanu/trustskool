# Sociedad Camper Review 2026: Free Spanish Community for Van Life Builders

Sociedad Camper is a free Skool community with 310 members, run in Spanish, welcoming anyone from complete beginners to experienced builders. The mission, in the group's own words, is to give members the community and support needed to build their "freedom on wheels."

The description explicitly spans two experience levels, "seas novato o experto" ("whether you're a novice or an expert"), which suggests a broad membership base rather than a course narrowly aimed at first-timers. The framing around "freedom on wheels" points to camper van conversion and nomadic living as the shared interest, with the community itself positioned as ongoing peer support rather than a one-off build guide. - Community and support framing suggests ongoing peer relationships rather than a single static resource.
