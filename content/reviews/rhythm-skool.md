## Who It's a Good Fit For

Musicians of any instrument who want to specifically strengthen their sense of rhythm and groove, rather than working on repertoire, theory, or technique in isolation from feel and timing. It's also a fit for musicians who play in bands or ensembles and want to lock in tighter with other players, since groove and timing are ultimately shared, interactive skills.
