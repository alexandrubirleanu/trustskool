# AI Automations by Jack Review 2026: Is It Worth $87/Month?

AI Automations by Jack is a 3,306-member Skool community teaching members to build AI automations, both for their own business and to sell as a service to others. It markets itself around unlocking "the exact AI systems behind my $100K/month AI business." Pricing sits at $87/month, with the community's own about page noting a planned increase to $97/month.

## What's Actually Inside

The core offer is access to what the community calls "Hermes OS," a full AI system stack, alongside Claude Code training and masterclass content covering both platforms with ready-to-use frameworks. The headline resource is a library of 110+ battle-tested automation blueprints spanning lead generation, content creation, and service delivery, built with tools like n8n, Claude Code, and Stripe. Beyond the blueprint library, members get daily live expert calls, one-on-one onboarding, unlimited tech support, and roughly $20,000 in stacked software discounts across various platforms tied to the community.

Multiple independent reviewers frame this community less as a course and more as delivery infrastructure, useful specifically to people already building or selling automations rather than people looking for a passive, from-scratch introduction to the field [(aifunnelinsider.com)](https://aifunnelinsider.com/ai-automations-by-jack-review-2026/).

## Pros

- **Deep blueprint library.** 110+ ready-to-use automation templates is a genuinely large practical resource compared to communities that lean more on theory.
- **Founder with a verifiable prior exit.** A documented startup track record before this community adds credibility beyond self-description.
- **Strong independent review consensus.** Skoolmakers and other review sites converge around a 4.3/5 rating with similar takeaways, rather than one outlier opinion.

## Cons

Independent reviewers are specific and consistent on one practical friction point: live calls run on a UK time schedule (roughly 10am-12pm BST), which means members in Asia or the Americas are mostly watching recordings rather than participating live [(aifunnelinsider.com)](https://aifunnelinsider.com/ai-automations-by-jack-review-2026/). Reviewers also frame it clearly as unsuited to beginners wanting a passive course, the value here is concentrated in active builders using the blueprint library for real client work, not in people looking to be taught automation concepts from zero.

## Who It's a Good Fit For

Agency builders and freelancers who already have some automation or client-delivery experience and want a large, ready-made template library plus daily support to speed up delivery. Reviewers note the fee tends to pay for itself within the first month for people actively using the community to land or fulfill client work.

## Who Should Skip It

Complete beginners looking for a slower, foundational introduction to AI automation will likely find the pace and framing mismatched, this is built for people already building, not people deciding whether to start. Members outside UK time zones should also go in aware that live-call participation will be limited compared to members in Europe, and should plan on catching up through recordings rather than expecting to ask questions live during peak sessions.

Anyone still choosing between a template-heavy delivery library and a slower, foundations-first curriculum should weigh which stage of their automation business they're actually at before paying for either.
