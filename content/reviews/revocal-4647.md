## What's Actually Inside

That geographic spread, from a Skool community to physical retreat locations across three regions, points to a hybrid model where the online space functions as both an ongoing daily practice hub and a pipeline toward deeper in-person immersion for members who want it. It also frames itself explicitly as a way to connect with fellow growth-oriented practitioners across regions, rather than a single-teacher, single-location studio community.

The recurring 12-day monthly UPA cadence is a real time commitment worth weighing against the free entry point: it's a meaningfully bigger ask than a passive content feed, closer to a standing practice schedule than a casual discussion group. Members who thrive on that kind of structure will likely get more out of it than someone hoping to dip in occasionally without a regular practice rhythm.
