# El Juego del Ecommerce Review 2026: Is This Free Community Worth Joining?

El Juego del Ecommerce™ is a free Spanish-language Skool community with 2,115 members aimed at existing online store founders. Its description is direct about the problem it addresses: moving "from irregular sales to a system that grows without a ceiling," with the explicit goal of generating real net profit in euros, not just top-line revenue.

## What's Actually Inside

The framing here targets founders already running a store but struggling with inconsistent sales, rather than people at the pre-launch stage. The emphasis on building a repeatable "system" and converting revenue into actual take-home profit suggests operational and profitability focus, as opposed to purely traffic-generation or ad-strategy content.

That distinction between top-line sales and actual net profit is a common gap in ecommerce education generally, since many founders can generate revenue through ads without ever building the operational systems, pricing discipline, or cost control needed to keep a meaningful share of it. A community explicitly framed around that gap is at least naming a real, widely discussed problem in the space.

## Pros

- **No cost to join**, letting existing store owners test the community's approach before any financial commitment.
- **Targets an existing pain point.** By naming "irregular sales" directly, the community speaks to founders already past the launch stage rather than beginners.
- **Profit-focused framing**, emphasizing net profit over vanity revenue metrics, which is a distinction many ecommerce communities skip.

## Cons

- The description doesn't specify platform focus (Shopify, marketplaces, or otherwise) or whether guidance covers paid ads, retention, or operations specifically.
- No independent reviews of this specific community were found beyond the platform listing.

## Who It's a Good Fit For

Existing online store founders, particularly in Spanish-speaking markets, dealing with inconsistent sales who want a systems-first approach to building predictable profit. Founders who have already tried scaling ad spend without seeing proportional profit growth are likely the clearest match for this systems-focused framing.

## Who Should Skip It

People who haven't yet launched a store and need pre-launch fundamentals, or non-Spanish speakers, will find this a poor fit. Founders already working with a dedicated ecommerce operations consultant may also find overlapping rather than additive value here.
