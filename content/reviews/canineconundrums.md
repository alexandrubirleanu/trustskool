## Cons

- No independent reviews found beyond the platform listing, so it's hard to verify the training philosophy or methods used without joining and seeing for yourself.
- A free community of this size may not substitute for one-on-one professional help with serious reactivity or aggression cases, which sometimes need in-person behavioral assessment.

## Who It's a Good Fit For

Owners dealing with a reactive, hard-to-recall, leash-pulling, or generally challenging dog, especially rescue dog owners navigating unknown history and adolescent dogs going through a regression phase, who want a free community focused specifically on these harder problems rather than basic obedience.

## Who Should Skip It

If your dog has already mastered the basics and you're looking for advanced sport, agility, or competition-level training rather than behavioral problem-solving, this community's problem-focused framing may not be the best match.
