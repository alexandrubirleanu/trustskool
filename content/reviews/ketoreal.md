## What's Actually Inside

The order of those three promises also tells you something about intended sequencing: hormonal health is named before the step-by-step plan, suggesting the community treats hormone balance as a prerequisite to sustainable fat loss rather than an afterthought bolted onto a standard keto meal plan. For women who've tried keto before and found it didn't stick, or found it worked short term and then reversed, that ordering is the part of the pitch worth paying closest attention to.

## Pros
