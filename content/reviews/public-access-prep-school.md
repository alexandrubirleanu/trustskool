# Public Access Prep School Review 2026: Free Psychiatric Service Dog Training Hub

Public Access Prep School is a free Skool community with 310 members built specifically around one task: training a future psychiatric service dog for public access work using R+, meaning reward-based, positive reinforcement methods.

## What's Actually Inside

Based on the community's own description, members get peer support and training plans aimed at preparing a dog for public access, the standard a service dog needs to meet to behave appropriately in public settings. The explicit mention of R+ signals a specific training philosophy, positive reinforcement rather than balanced or aversive correction-based methods, which matters to owner-trainers who've already chosen that approach and want a community that shares it rather than debates it.

## Pros

- Free to join, which lowers the barrier for owner-trainers already managing the real costs of raising a service dog prospect.
- A specific, stated training philosophy (R+) means less risk of getting conflicting advice from members using incompatible methods.
- Training plans plus peer support suggests practical structure rather than just open discussion.

## Who Runs It

No founder or trainer name is listed in the public record reviewed.

## Cons

- The description doesn't specify the dog's task training beyond public access work, so it's unclear how much of the group covers psychiatric-specific tasks versus general manners.
- No independent reviews were found beyond the platform listing.

## Who It's a Good Fit For

Owner-trainers working on a psychiatric service dog prospect who already use, or want to learn, reward-based positive reinforcement methods and want peer accountability plus ready-made training plans along the way.

## Who Should Skip It

Anyone using or committed to balanced or correction-based training methods will likely find friction with the group's R+ focus. People looking to acquire an already-trained service dog, rather than owner-train one from scratch, are also outside the scope of what this community appears built for.
