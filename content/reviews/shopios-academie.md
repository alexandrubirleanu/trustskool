# Shopify Académie (par ShopiOS) Review 2026: Is This Free Skool Community Worth Joining?

Shopify Académie, run under the ShopiOS name, is a free Skool community with 1,775 members built specifically for French-speaking Shopify store owners. Its own description positions it as "la communauté Shopify numéro #1 pour apprendre, échanger, s'entraider et progresser avec ton activité sur Shopify" (the number-one Shopify community to learn, exchange, help each other, and grow your Shopify business).

## What's Actually Inside

The description points to four connected activities: learning Shopify, exchanging ideas with other members, mutual help, and ongoing progress on an existing Shopify business. That combination suggests a community built for people who already run or are actively launching a Shopify store, rather than complete beginners exploring e-commerce as a concept. Because it's explicitly French-language, the natural fit is French-speaking entrepreneurs, whether based in France, Quebec, Belgium, or elsewhere in the francophone e-commerce world.

Because the community operates specifically for Shopify rather than e-commerce platforms generally, members running stores on Shopify's competitors, such as WooCommerce or BigCommerce, won't find directly applicable troubleshooting here even if broader e-commerce principles overlap. That specificity is generally a strength for an active Shopify seller, since platform-specific quirks around apps, themes, and checkout flows rarely translate cleanly across platforms.

## Pros

- Free membership with a clearly defined niche, Shopify specifically rather than e-commerce in general, which tends to produce more relevant peer discussion than broad marketplaces.
- Explicit emphasis on mutual help ("s'entraider") suggests an active, participatory culture rather than a one-way content feed.
- Serves an underserved language segment, since most large Shopify communities operate primarily in English.

## Cons

- Non-French speakers will get limited value here, since both the community's positioning and presumably its day-to-day discussion run in French.
- The "#1" claim in the description is the community's own positioning rather than an independently verified ranking, so it's worth treating as marketing language rather than a confirmed fact.

## Who It's a Good Fit For

French-speaking Shopify store owners, at any stage from setup to scaling, who want peer support and practical troubleshooting from others running similar stores in the same language.

## Who Should Skip It

Non-French speakers, and anyone looking for platform-agnostic e-commerce education (Amazon, Etsy, WooCommerce, and similar) rather than Shopify-specific help, should look elsewhere.
