# E-Commerce For All Review 2026: Is It Worth $39 a Month?

E-Commerce For All is a 349-member, Arabic-language Skool community priced at $39 a month. Its description, translated, calls itself "the e-commerce community for everyone" and claims to be "the first e-commerce community that provides you with personal training from zero to professional."

## What's Actually Inside

The "zero to professional" framing suggests a progression-based curriculum rather than a single-level course, meant to take complete beginners through to a more advanced stage rather than assuming existing e-commerce experience. The word "personal" alongside training implies some individualized element, one-on-one feedback or personalized guidance, rather than a purely generic, one-size-fits-all video course, though the description doesn't detail exactly what that personalization looks like in practice.

The claim to be "the first" e-commerce community of its kind is a positioning statement rather than something we could verify, and it's common for marketing copy in a crowded education space to lean on being first or unique. That claim is worth setting aside when evaluating the community and focusing instead on the two things that are concrete, a structured beginner-to-professional path and some form of personal training.

## Pros

- A clearly stated beginner-to-advanced progression rather than content aimed at only one skill level.
- Arabic-language content specifically built for Arabic-speaking entrepreneurs, addressing a market where much e-commerce education is English-first.
- The word "personal" in the description suggests some individualized support beyond passive video lessons.

## Cons

The description doesn't specify which e-commerce model, dropshipping, private label, marketplace selling, is taught, so it's worth confirming the specific approach matches your goals. We also found no independent reviews of this community beyond its own Skool listing.

## Who It's a Good Fit For

Arabic-speaking beginners to e-commerce who want a structured, progression-based path with some personalized support, and who are comfortable with a $39 monthly commitment.

## Who Should Skip It

Experienced e-commerce sellers looking for advanced, specialized tactics rather than a beginner-to-professional foundational path may find this too introductory for their current level.
