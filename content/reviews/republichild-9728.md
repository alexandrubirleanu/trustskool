## What's Actually Inside

The description points to three concrete pillars: awareness (conscience), practical tools (outils), and ongoing support (soutien), all aimed at parents who feel screens have come between them and their kids. That word choice also suggests the community treats screen habits as a symptom of a broader connection problem to address, rather than the whole issue in itself.

The name itself, "Cercle CONNEXION" (Connection Circle), reinforces the core promise before a reader even gets to the longer description, which suggests the branding and the actual content are meant to reinforce the same single idea rather than a broad, multi-topic parenting curriculum.
