## What's Actually Inside

The core distinguishing claim here is "fully digital," positioning this as an online-only alternative or complement to traditional in-person flight schools. Aviation training typically combines theoretical ground school, covering regulations, navigation, meteorology, and aircraft systems, with practical flight hours, and a "fully digital" framing suggests the theoretical portion, and possibly exam preparation, is delivered through this Skool community, though actual flight hours would necessarily still require in-person instruction and aircraft access.

## Cons

- No independent reviews of this specific community were found beyond the Skool platform listing, and "fully digital" flight training still requires clarifying how practical flight hours and official certification exams are handled.

## Who It's a Good Fit For
