## Pros

- **Very low barrier to entry.** At $7/month, it's cheap to test whether the community fits before committing further.
- **Sizable member base.** 1,100 members suggests genuine activity rather than an empty shell group.

The public description is minimal, three words, which makes it hard to judge in advance whether members get structured lessons, a content vault, live sessions, or mostly an open discussion feed.
