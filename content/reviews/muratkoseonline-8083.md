# Meta Reklam Atölyesi Review 2026: Free Turkish Community for Running Your Own Meta Ads

Meta Reklam Atölyesi (Meta Ads Workshop, in Turkish) is a free Skool community with 330 members, built for Turkish-speaking business owners and marketers who currently outsource their Meta advertising to an agency and want to bring that skill in-house instead.

## What's Actually Inside

The community's own description is direct about the problem it addresses: stop leaving your Meta ads to an agency, and manage them yourself. To support that shift, it points to three components, templates members can use directly, community support for troubleshooting, and what it calls a field-tested system, implying the approach has been used in real campaigns rather than being purely theoretical. The stated end goals are equally concrete: stop wasting budget and increase ROAS (return on ad spend), which are the two metrics that matter most to anyone running paid Meta campaigns.

## Who Runs It

No owner name is listed for this community in the data reviewed, so this review does not cover any individual's advertising background or agency experience, despite the slug referencing a personal name.

## Pros

- Free to join, letting business owners test whether in-housing their Meta ads management is realistic before committing further time or money.
- Templates plus community support plus a stated system suggests a fairly complete toolkit rather than scattered tips.
- Clear, business-relevant end goals (reduce wasted budget, raise ROAS) rather than vague promises about "growing your ads."

## Cons

- No independent reviews found beyond the platform listing, so the "field-tested" claim and actual ROAS improvements cannot be independently verified.
- The description does not specify ad spend levels or industries the templates were tested on, which matters since ad strategy can vary significantly by budget size and niche.

## Who It's a Good Fit For

Turkish-speaking business owners or marketers currently paying an agency for Meta ads who want to learn to manage campaigns themselves, particularly if budget waste has been a concern.

## Who Should Skip It

Businesses happy with their current agency relationship, or those running ads on platforms other than Meta, are unlikely to find the templates and system directly applicable.
