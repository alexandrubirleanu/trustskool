## What's Actually Inside

The description points to a recurring, live format, weekly co-working, rather than a static library of lessons. People who already use AI tools somewhat regularly but struggle to carve out consistent time to apply them, and who want a scheduled, communal work session rather than another self-paced course to work through alone.
