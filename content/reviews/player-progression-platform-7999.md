Communities built by current or former players rather than professional content marketers often carry a different flavor: less produced, more like locker-room advice passed on directly. That can be a real asset for players who want practical, experience-based tips, though it also means the content is likely less polished or systematically organized than a paid academy course with a dedicated curriculum team.
