# Outcomes Academy Review 2026: A Free Personal Branding Community With 1:1 Support

Outcomes Academy is a free, French-language Skool community with 691 members. Its own description sets out a specific goal: building a personal brand that attracts, influences, and monetizes, paired with what it describes as strategic 1:1 accompaniment from Kael Cruz.

## What's Actually Inside

The description names three connected outcomes for a personal brand, attraction, influence, and monetization, treating them as a sequence rather than separate goals: first drawing attention, then building enough trust to have influence, and finally converting that into revenue. The mention of "un accompagnement stratégique 1:1," strategic 1:1 support, distinguishes this from a purely self-paced course, suggesting at least some individualized guidance is part of the offer alongside whatever group content exists inside the Skool space. Being free while offering 1:1 support is a notable combination, since many communities that mention individualized coaching sit behind a paywall. Personal branding as a category tends to attract people at very different stages, some with an existing audience looking to monetize, others starting from nothing, so how well the "attract, influence, monetize" sequence works for any given member will likely depend heavily on where they're starting from before joining.

## Pros

- Free to join, despite the description mentioning individualized 1:1 support as part of the offer.
- Covers the full arc from personal brand building to monetization, rather than stopping at growth alone.
- Structured, sequential framing (attract, influence, monetize) gives a clear roadmap logic to the stated approach.

## Cons

The description doesn't detail how the 1:1 support is allocated, whether every member gets it or it's limited, so it's worth clarifying that directly before joining with that expectation. We also found no independent reviews of this specific community beyond its own Skool listing, so it's worth asking directly how the free tier and 1:1 support actually interact before joining.

## Who It's a Good Fit For

French-speaking creators or entrepreneurs looking to build and monetize a personal brand, and who want at least some individualized guidance alongside community content, fit the stated pitch.

## Who Should Skip It

Anyone looking for a purely self-paced course with no personal interaction may find the 1:1 framing unnecessary for their needs. Non-French speakers will also want to confirm the language before joining. Being free removes most of the downside of testing that fit directly rather than deciding from the pitch alone.
