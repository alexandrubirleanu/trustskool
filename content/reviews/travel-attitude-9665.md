# Travel Attitude Review 2026: Free Community for Travel + Business

It's a free, French-language community with 1,084 members aimed at people who want to build a business and travel at the same time, without the traditional trade-off between the two. The description explicitly frames right now as the moment to act differently, positioning the community as much around momentum and mindset as around any specific tactical business skill.

## What's Actually Inside

The description leans heavily on lifestyle and mindset language, living, learning, and evolving "without limits", which points to a community organized more around a shared aspiration than a single, narrowly defined curriculum. Expect discussion likely spanning remote or location-independent business building, travel experiences, and the mindset side of pursuing both simultaneously, though the exact structure (courses versus open discussion) isn't spelled out in the public listing. Communities built around a lifestyle goal rather than a single skill tend to work best as a source of motivation and peer connection, with any concrete business tactics likely supplementing that core identity rather than replacing it.

## Pros

- **Free to join.** No cost to explore whether the travel-and-business lifestyle angle resonates.
- **Clear dual focus.** Combining travel and entrepreneurship gives the community a distinct identity versus a generic business or travel group alone.
- **Reasonable existing size.** 1,084 members suggests a genuinely active peer group.
- **Motivational framing at no cost.** For people early in figuring out a location-independent lifestyle, free access to a like-minded peer group can itself be a useful starting point.

## Cons

The description is largely aspirational and motivational in tone rather than specific about deliverables, so it's hard to know from the listing alone whether members get structured business training, travel logistics guidance, or mostly community and inspiration. We also found no independent reviews of this specific community beyond its own Skool listing.

## Who It's a Good Fit For

French-speaking people actively trying to build location-independent income or a business that supports travel, who want a community of others pursuing the same combined lifestyle.

## Who Should Skip It

Anyone wanting a strictly business-focused or strictly travel-planning community may find the combined framing less targeted than a single-topic group. People needing concrete, step-by-step business training should confirm that's actually part of the offering before joining.
