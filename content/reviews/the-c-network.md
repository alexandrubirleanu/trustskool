# The C* Network Review 2026: Is $37/Month Worth It?

At $37 a month, The C* Network offers a Spanish-language space with 1,230 members centered on personal growth and connection. The community's own description frames it as "un espacio seguro" (a safe space) where a guide named Andy teaches members "to grow, connect, and create with direction, without getting lost, and enjoying the process."

## What's Actually Inside

The description emphasizes direction and process as much as outcome: growth and connection are the stated goals, but the "sin perderte" (without losing yourself) and "disfrutando el proceso" (enjoying the process) language suggests a slower, more relationship-and-mindset-oriented approach than a pure tactics-and-hustle community. Sessions appear to be guided directly, based on the framing around Andy leading members through the material, rather than a self-serve library members work through alone.

## Pros

- A clearly stated "safe space" framing, which signals an emphasis on supportive community over a purely competitive or sales-heavy vibe.
- Spanish-language delivery serves a market segment often underserved by English-first growth communities.
- Direct guidance built into the pitch rather than a pure content-dump structure.
- The emphasis on process, not just outcomes, may suit members who've burned out on hustle-culture communities that skip straight to tactics.

## Cons

The description doesn't specify session format, frequency, or exactly what "creating with direction" involves in practice, so the day-to-day experience is somewhat unclear from the public listing alone. We also found no independent reviews of this specific community beyond its Skool page.

## Who It's a Good Fit For

Spanish-speaking members looking for a guided, relationship-focused approach to personal growth and connection, who value process and support as much as concrete outcomes.

## Who Should Skip It

Anyone looking for a purely tactical, metrics-driven business or marketing community will likely find the growth-and-connection framing here too broad, and English speakers won't get the full experience of a Spanish-first space. People uncomfortable with a coach-led format, where one person's direction shapes the group's process, may also prefer a more peer-to-peer or self-directed community instead.

At $37 a month, it's a mid-range price for this kind of coached community, which makes it reasonable to look at how the membership has actually trended before deciding whether the "safe space" framing holds up in practice.
