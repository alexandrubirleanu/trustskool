# Channeling Community Review 2026: Free German Spiritual Space Worth It?

Channeling Community is a free German-language Skool group with 1,019 members, built around what its description calls a "lichtvoller Raum," a light-filled space, for connecting with like-minded people on a deep spiritual level. The closing line of its own pitch, "WIR SIND VIELE!" ("We are many!"), leans into the idea of a large, gathered community rather than a small private circle.

## What's Actually Inside

The description centers on three things: networking with like-minded people, exchanging on a deep spiritual level, and growing together. Channeling, as a practice, typically refers to accessing or communicating messages believed to come from beyond ordinary consciousness, spirit guides, higher selves, or similar concepts depending on the tradition, so the community likely revolves around discussion, shared experiences, and possibly group sessions related to that practice, rather than secular self-help content. The emphasis on "vernetzen" (networking/connecting) suggests member interaction is a core part of the offer, not just one-way content from an organizer.

For people practicing channeling, finding others who share the same frame of reference can matter more than in most self-help niches, since the practice tends to be met with skepticism outside dedicated spiritual circles. A community explicitly built as a "lichtvoller Raum" (light-filled space) signals an intentionally accepting, non-judgmental environment for that kind of exchange, which is likely a meaningful part of the draw for members beyond whatever specific techniques or content get shared.

## Pros

- Free to join, so German-speaking members interested in channeling and spiritual practice can explore the community without financial commitment.
- Explicitly community-oriented language ("we are many," "connect with like-minded people") suggests an active peer-to-peer culture rather than a passive content feed.
- Serves the German-speaking spiritual/channeling niche, which has fewer dedicated Skool communities than English-language equivalents.

## Cons

- The description doesn't specify format, whether there are live group sessions, one-on-one channeling, or purely text-based discussion.
- No independent reviews of this community were found beyond its own Skool listing, and spiritual/esoteric claims in general are inherently not verifiable through outside review.

## Who It's a Good Fit For

German speakers with an existing interest in channeling and spiritual practice who want a free community to connect with others exploring the same path.

## Who Should Skip It

Anyone skeptical of or uninterested in channeling and spiritual practice as a framework clearly won't find this a fit. Non-German speakers should look for an equivalent community in their own language instead.
