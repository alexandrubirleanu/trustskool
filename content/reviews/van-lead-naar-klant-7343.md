# De AI Professional Review 2026: Free Dutch Skool Community for AI-Ready Careers

De AI Professional is a free, Dutch-language Skool community with 711 members. Its description frames a specific, time-sensitive premise: that the gap created by AI adoption will widen over the next two years, and members should use that window to build what it calls their own "schaduwteam," a shadow team of AI tools, to become the kind of professional who actually puts AI to work.

## What's Actually Inside

The stated focus is helping working professionals move from AI awareness to practical AI application in their day-to-day work, building a personal system or "shadow team" of AI tools rather than treating AI as a side curiosity. The framing suggests urgency, positioning the next two years as a critical window for professionals to adapt, which implies content geared toward practical, job-relevant AI use rather than purely technical or developer-focused material.

The "schaduwteam" concept, essentially assembling a personal stack of AI tools that function like an extra set of hands on repetitive or time-consuming tasks, has become a common framing across professional AI-adoption communities generally, since it gives non-technical professionals a concrete mental model for what "using AI at work" actually looks like day to day. Without more detail on which tools or workflows are covered, it's the underlying philosophy rather than a specific curriculum that's clear from the listing.

- Framed around practical, workplace application of AI rather than abstract theory or coding.
- Clear urgency framing, a widening gap over two years, may help motivate consistent engagement rather than passive lurking.

## Cons

## Who Should Skip It
