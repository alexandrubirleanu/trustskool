Mission Reprise & Business is a free French-language Skool community with 485 members built around a specific and fairly bold acquisition strategy: taking over a profitable company generating between 1 and 10 million euros in revenue, with zero euros of personal capital invested, even for people with no prior experience or much free time.

## What's Actually Inside

The pitch centers on business acquisition, a well-established path in the French business world for buying an existing profitable company rather than starting one from scratch, but the community's specific angle is doing it with no money down. That typically implies structuring deals around seller financing, earnouts, or leveraged structures rather than paying cash upfront, and the "even without experience, even with little time" framing suggests the content is aimed at people outside the traditional M&A or private equity world.

Business acquisition, called reprise d'entreprise locally, is an established enough concept in the French business world to have its own dedicated vocabulary and support ecosystem, distinct from the startup-founding path most entrepreneurship content defaults to. A community built specifically around that acquisition route, rather than treating it as a footnote to "how to start a business" content, is at least organizing itself around a real and named alternative path rather than inventing a niche from nothing.
