## What's Actually Inside

This falls into the "prop firm funding" niche, where traders pay an evaluation fee to a proprietary trading firm, then trade a demo account under specific rules, and if they pass, get access to a funded account and split profits with the firm. The description's mention of a trading bot "mientras aprendes," while you learn, suggests some automated or semi-automated trading tool is part of the offer, alongside the educational content on passing funding challenges. Prop-firm evaluations typically impose strict rules around drawdown limits, daily loss caps, and profit targets within a fixed timeframe, and coaching built specifically around passing those evaluations, as opposed to general trading education, focuses on rule adherence and risk management under those constraints as much as on trading strategy itself.

## Who It's a Good Fit For
