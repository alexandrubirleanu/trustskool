# Sztuka Notowania Review 2026: What's Inside This Polish Note-Taking Community?

Sztuka Notowania ("The Art of Note-Taking") is a free, 621-member Polish-language Skool community, but there's an important catch stated right in its own description: it's only for people who've already taken a paid course. The group itself doesn't cost anything to join on Skool, yet access is gated to alumni of a separate paid training. That distinction matters for anyone evaluating it purely by the listed price.

## What's Actually Inside

Per the community's description, the group exists to help members build personal knowledge bases, learn from each other, and work together to improve their notes. That points to a peer-learning structure focused on personal knowledge management rather than a one-way course feed, members are expected to both take in techniques and actively refine their own note-taking systems alongside others who bought the same course.

## Pros

- Being restricted to paid-course alumni means the group is likely made up of people who share a common baseline and vocabulary, which tends to raise discussion quality.
- The collaborative framing ("we work together on better notes") suggests active peer feedback rather than passive content consumption.
- Free access on Skool itself removes a second cost layer on top of whatever the underlying course already charged.

## Cons

- It's not open to the general public, so anyone curious about the specific note-taking method has to find and purchase the separate paid course first, which isn't described here.
- The listing doesn't name the course, its price, or its instructor, so there's no way to judge the underlying methodology from this description alone, and no independent reviews were found.

## Who It's a Good Fit For

Polish speakers who have already purchased the associated paid note-taking course and want an ongoing peer space to keep refining their system with people who learned the same method.

## Who Should Skip It

Anyone who hasn't taken the underlying paid course, non-Polish speakers, or people looking for a free-standing, open note-taking community they can join without a prerequisite purchase.
