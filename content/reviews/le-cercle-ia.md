## What's Actually Inside

The audience targeting here is notable: this isn't pitched at general AI enthusiasts, it's aimed squarely at people running businesses or teams who want time back. The three components named, formations (training), automatisations (automations), and communauté (community), suggest a structure that combines learning AI tools, actually implementing automation in a leader's workflow, and having peers to compare notes with, rather than just consuming AI news. For a leader with limited hours to spare, that combination matters more than raw content volume, the implementation and peer-support pieces are what typically turn AI theory into actual reclaimed time on someone's calendar.
