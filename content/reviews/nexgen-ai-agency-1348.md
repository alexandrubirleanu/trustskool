# Apex AI Automation Academy Review 2026: Worth It for German Speakers?

There's no shortage of English-language AI automation communities on Skool, which makes Apex AI Automation Academy stand out mostly for its audience: a free, German-language community of 1,457 members built around learning AI-powered automation tools and turning that skill into an actual automation business. At that size, it's a reasonably established option within the smaller pool of German-language AI communities rather than a brand-new experiment with a handful of early members.

## What's Actually Inside

The community's own description frames two things happening at once. First, learning the AI-powered tools used for workflow automation itself. Second, and arguably the more distinctive part, using those tools to build an automation business, with the stated goal of taking members "from zero to first client" (von null zum ersten Kunden). That framing suggests the community measures progress less by tool fluency alone and more by whether members actually land paying automation work. It also implies a certain amount of business-side coaching alongside the technical training, since finding and closing that first client is a sales problem as much as a tooling one.

## Pros

- Free to join, which lowers the bar for German speakers curious about AI automation as a business rather than just a personal productivity trick.
- A dual focus on tool skills and client acquisition, rather than teaching automation software in a vacuum with no path to income.
- Native German-language community, useful for anyone who'd rather learn in their own language than translate English-first automation content.

## Cons

The description doesn't name specific tools or platforms taught (whether that's Make, Zapier, n8n, or something proprietary), so it's worth confirming the tech stack matches what you already use or want to learn before joining. We also didn't find independent reviews of this specific community beyond its own Skool listing, so member experience with the "first client" milestone specifically is something to ask about directly before joining.

## Who It's a Good Fit For

German-speaking beginners who want to learn AI automation tools with an explicit eye toward freelancing or agency work, particularly anyone motivated by the concrete milestone of landing a first client rather than automation as an abstract skill to collect.

## Who Should Skip It

English-only speakers will likely find more community options elsewhere, and anyone already running an established automation agency may find "zero to first client" material too foundational for where they're at.
