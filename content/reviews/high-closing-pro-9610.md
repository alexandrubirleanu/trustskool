# L'ACADÉMIE DES TOP CLOSERS Review 2026: Is This Free Sales Program Worth It?

L'ACADÉMIE DES TOP CLOSERS is a free, French-language Skool community with 384 members, marketed as a complete program ("Programme Complet") aimed at making members the top salesperson at their company. The stated goal is ambitious and specific: mastering the closing secrets of the best salespeople to generate five-figure months and up, which sets a fairly high bar for what "success" inside the community is supposed to look like.

## What's Actually Inside

Based on the description, the program is structured around closing technique specifically, the part of the sales process where a prospect actually commits to buying, rather than earlier-stage skills like prospecting or lead generation. The "top vendeur" (top salesperson) framing and the five-figure monthly income target suggest the content is aimed at commission-based or high-ticket sales roles where closing ability directly and visibly affects income, rather than salaried roles with less direct performance-to-pay correlation.

## Pros

- Free access to what's described as a full program, lowering the barrier for salespeople curious about improving their closing skills.
- Sharp focus specifically on closing technique rather than a broad, unfocused "sales training" umbrella.
- French-language delivery serves French-speaking sales professionals directly.
- Clear stated benchmark (top performer, five-figure months) gives members a concrete way to measure whether the program is working for them.

## Cons

- No independent reviews found beyond the platform listing, so the "five-figure months" outcome and the closing techniques taught can't be independently verified.
- Ambitious income framing in sales community marketing is common and doesn't guarantee individual results, which depend heavily on the seller's product, market, and existing skill level.

## Who It's a Good Fit For

Salespeople already working in commission-driven or high-ticket sales roles who specifically want to sharpen their closing technique and are motivated by the stated goal of becoming their company's top performer, especially those who feel their current closing rate doesn't match the volume of conversations they're having.

## Who Should Skip It

Anyone earlier in their sales career still building foundational prospecting and qualification skills, or those working in lower-ticket or salaried sales roles where closing technique alone won't move income much, may find a broader sales fundamentals resource more useful than a closing-specific program. Non-French speakers would also need to work around the language barrier to get value here.
