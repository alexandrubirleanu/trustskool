## What's Actually Inside

The equipment list in the description (just a ball, cones, and grass) signals that this is individual technical skill training designed to be done outside of formal team practice, likely footwork drills, ball mastery routines, and touch-and-control exercises that a player can run through alone. The 90-day, money-back-guaranteed framing suggests a structured progression rather than a loose library of random drill videos, with a defined start and end point members can measure themselves against.

## Pros

- Low monthly price ($22) relative to many sports skills programs, lowering the risk of trying it
- Explicit money-back guarantee on a specific outcome, which is a stronger commitment than most fitness or skills communities make
- Minimal equipment requirement removes a common barrier (no access to a gym, coach, or expensive gear needed)

## Cons

- "Most technical player on your team" is a relative, self-assessed outcome that's hard to measure objectively, and the description doesn't explain how progress or the guarantee itself is actually evaluated
- No independent reviews were found beyond the platform listing, so real refund experiences and typical outcomes can't be verified

## Who It's a Good Fit For

Youth or amateur soccer players who want structured, low-cost individual technical training they can do outside of team practice, and who are motivated enough to follow a self-directed program consistently.

## Who Should Skip It

Players looking for tactical or team-based coaching, rather than individual technical skill work, or those who need in-person feedback to stay motivated, may find a self-guided program like this a harder fit.
