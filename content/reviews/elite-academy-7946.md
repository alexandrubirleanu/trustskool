## What's Actually Inside

Going by the description, the community structures itself around a business-building arc for people already working in or entering the wellness space: starting with mindset, moving through strategy, then the practical mechanics of acquisition, bringing in clients, conversion, turning interest into paying clients, and fidélisation, retention. That's a fairly complete funnel covered under one roof, which suggests the intended member is someone running or launching a wellness-based offer who needs the business side handled alongside the craft itself.

Many wellness practitioners are trained deeply in their craft, coaching, nutrition, movement, therapy, but not in running the business side of turning that expertise into a sustainable income. Bundling mindset work with the concrete mechanics of client acquisition and retention suggests the community is trying to address that specific gap directly, rather than assuming members already know how to price, market, or retain clients once they've built the skill itself.
