## Who It's a Good Fit For

Dutch-speaking beginners who want a no-cost introduction to dropshipping and are comfortable learning in a large, high-membership group rather than a small, closely mentored one.

## Who Should Skip It

Non-Dutch speakers will run into a real language barrier here, since the community's description and presumably its content and discussions are in Dutch. Anyone looking for an English-language dropshipping community, or for advanced, one-on-one style coaching rather than free general video content, should also look elsewhere.
