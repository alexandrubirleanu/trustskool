# AI VAULT Review 2026: Is It Worth $97/Month?

AI VAULT is a 3,678-member Skool community that describes itself as "the #1 AI OFM community for tools, training, and results." OFM stands for OnlyFans Management, and the community teaches members to create and operate AI-generated influencer personas that monetize on subscription content platforms. It's a more specific and higher-friction niche than most communities in this batch, worth naming plainly rather than glossing over, since it shapes who this is actually for. Current pricing sits at $97/month, described on the about page as a promotional rate before a scheduled increase to $299.

## What's Actually Inside

The centerpiece is a 7-week program built around generating a first $5,000 in online revenue using synthetic AI personas, which the community values at $997 on its own. Around that core program sit several bundled components: a dedicated support community (valued at $497), an "Uncensored ComfyUI AI Automations" toolkit for generating persona content (valued at $997), and a "Successful AI Influencers Exclusive Masterclass" (also valued at $997). Those bundle valuations are the community's own marketing figures rather than independently appraised, standard practice for offer stacking in this space but worth reading as promotional framing rather than fixed market value.

The program includes a 60-day money-back guarantee tied to results, meaning members who don't hit revenue targets within that window can request a refund per the stated policy.

## Who Runs It

Mathieu Juanico is described across multiple course-distribution and marketing sites as a digital entrepreneur and "automation architect" specializing in AI workflows [(coursecook.com)](https://coursecook.com/ai-vault-mathieu-juanico/) [(wsodl.com)](https://wsodl.com/course/mathieu-juanico-ai-vault/). Most of what's publicly findable about him comes from course marketing and resale/download sites rather than independent press, professional profiles, or third-party journalism, which is a meaningfully thinner verification trail than several other founders in this review batch.

## Pros

- **Specific, focused business model.** Rather than vague "make money with AI" framing, the community teaches one clearly defined workflow end to end.
- **Results-based refund window.** A 60-day guarantee tied to hitting a revenue target is a more concrete commitment than a standard satisfaction-based refund policy.
- **Bundled toolkit.** Members reportedly get both the training and the technical tooling (ComfyUI automations) needed to execute, rather than training alone.

## Cons

This is the one community in this batch where we could not verify the founder's background beyond promotional and course-resale sites, and where independent, non-marketing reviews were essentially absent from our research. The AI-influencer-for-OnlyFans business model also carries platform-policy and reputational risk that's structurally different from the other niches reviewed here: subscription content platforms and AI-generation tools both evolve their content and disclosure policies frequently, and a workflow built around today's rules may need real rework if those policies shift. Anyone considering this should go in with clear eyes about both the founder-verification gap and the platform-dependency risk.

## Who It's a Good Fit For

Someone who has specifically decided to pursue AI-influencer content monetization on subscription platforms, understands the platform-policy risk inherent to that model, and wants a structured program plus tooling rather than figuring out the technical stack from scratch.

## Who Should Skip It

Anyone uncomfortable with the specific business model, or looking for general AI content or automation skills applicable across different projects, will find a better fit in one of the broader AI content or automation communities in this niche. Given the thinner independent verification trail on the founder, more risk-averse buyers should look for documented member outcomes beyond the community's own marketing before committing at the $97 (soon $299) price point.
