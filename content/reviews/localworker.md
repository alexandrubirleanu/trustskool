# Community für Dienstleister Review 2026: Free German Local Services Group

Community für Dienstleister ("community for service providers") is a free Skool group with 535 members, run in German for owners of local service businesses. Its pitch is direct: more customers, more revenue, and fewer mistakes through practical knowledge and shared experience, rather than abstract marketing theory.

## What's Actually Inside

The description frames this as coming from lived experience: "meine Erfahrungen, meine Siege" (my experiences, my wins), suggesting content built around the founder's own results running or advising local service businesses. Expect practical, ground-level material aimed at owners of trades and local services, things like customer acquisition tactics, avoiding common operational mistakes, and revenue growth approaches specific to service-based businesses rather than e-commerce or SaaS.

## Pros

- Free to join, so local business owners can test the value before spending anything
- Positioned around real, lived operator experience rather than purely theoretical marketing content
- Focused niche (local service providers) means advice is more likely to be directly applicable than generic small-business content

## Cons

- The description doesn't name specific trades or service types covered, so it's unclear how relevant the content is to any one type of local business
- No independent reviews were found beyond the platform listing, so the "wins" referenced in the description can't be independently verified

## Who It's a Good Fit For

Owners of local service businesses in German-speaking markets who want practical, experience-based advice on customer acquisition and avoiding operational mistakes, and who prefer learning from a peer group over formal courses.

## Who Should Skip It

Non-German speakers, or business owners outside local services (e-commerce, digital products, and similar) will likely find the content less applicable to their situation.
