# SheProfits Review 2026: Is This Free Women's Ecommerce Community Worth It?

SheProfits is a free, English-language Skool community with 568 members that describes itself as the number one women's ecommerce community, teaching members how to launch, grow, and profit from both physical and digital products.

## What's Actually Inside

The dual focus on physical and digital products makes this a broader umbrella than a single-model community like a pure dropshipping or print-on-demand group. Combined with the women-specific framing, the implication is a peer-support angle alongside tactical ecommerce content, aimed at an audience that may want a shared community context as much as the business tactics themselves. Because the description doesn't commit to one go-to business model, members may end up seeing a mix of approaches rather than deep specialization in a single one.

## Pros

- Free to join, removing cost as a barrier to exploring the community.
- Women-focused community and support structure may appeal to those wanting a specific peer network, not just tactical content.
- Broad scope across physical and digital products gives multiple potential entry points for people still deciding on a business model.

## Cons

- The "#1 women's ecommerce community" claim is self-described rather than independently verified.
- Covering both physical and digital products could mean shallower depth on any single business model compared with a more narrowly focused community, and no independent reviews were found beyond the platform's own listing.

## Who It's a Good Fit For

Women new to ecommerce who want a supportive, women-centered community while they're still exploring whether physical or digital products fit them best.

## Who Should Skip It

People who've already chosen a specific ecommerce model and want deep, narrow expertise in just that model, or anyone who doesn't need a gender-specific community framing.
