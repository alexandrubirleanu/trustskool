The page credits Minozan as the presenter behind the community. It's a more technical spin on the classic agency-building model that's common on Skool, shifting the service from generic marketing or content work toward AI infrastructure specifically.

## What's Actually Inside

The core promise is narrow and direct: help members launch their own agency built around AI infrastructure services. The public description doesn't define exactly what "AI infrastructure" services entail in practice, whether that's setting up automation pipelines, integrating AI tools into client operations, or something more technical still, so the specific service offering members would be selling isn't fully spelled out. What is clear is the positioning: this is an agency-building community with a specific technical angle rather than a general "start an SMMA" course aimed at content or ads.

## Pros

- Free to join, with no stated cost barrier.
- A more technical, specific angle, AI infrastructure, than the generic social-media-agency communities that dominate this space.
- French-language, serving an audience that's less saturated with agency-building content than the English-language market.

## Cons

The description doesn't define what "AI infrastructure" services actually look like day to day, so prospective members can't fully evaluate whether it matches their existing skills. We also found no independent reviews of this community.

## Who It's a Good Fit For

French speakers interested in starting an agency with a technical, AI-infrastructure angle rather than a generic marketing or content agency, and who are comfortable learning the specifics of that service model as they go. It's also worth a look for people already running a generic agency who want to add a more technical, AI-related service line.

## Who Should Skip It

Non-French speakers won't get direct value here. People specifically wanting a traditional social-media or content marketing agency model, rather than an AI-infrastructure-focused one, are likely a better fit for a different community.
