The community's own framing describes it as a space for business development through video content. ## Who It's a Good Fit For

Bulgarian-speaking creators or small business owners who want to use video content specifically as a growth channel and are looking for a low-cost way to test a niche community. It should also suit creators who already have some video output and want a community specifically focused on turning that into a business, rather than starting from a blank camera roll.
