Going strictly off the description, "Bana kazandıran yapay zeka otomasyonlarını kopyala ve çalıştır" ("Copy and run the AI automations that make me money"), the promise is access to specific automation workflows framed as already proven by the creator's own use, rather than general AI theory or coding lessons. It reads as a templates-and-workflows offer aimed at people who want to replicate a working system rather than build one from scratch.

## Pros

- Direct, outcome-focused pitch, copy working automations, rather than a vague "learn AI" promise.

## Cons

- The description doesn't name which platforms, tools, or automation stacks are involved, so buyers won't know the technical footprint until after joining.
- We found no independent reviews or verification of the earnings implied by the community's own money-making claims.

Turkish-speaking founders or freelancers who want ready-to-copy AI automation templates rather than a from-scratch education in AI or coding.

## Who Should Skip It

Anyone who wants to understand the underlying logic of automations rather than just deploy templates, or non-Turkish speakers, will likely find better fits elsewhere.
