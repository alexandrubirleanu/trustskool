# Optionentreff Review 2026: Free German Options Trading Community for Women

Optionentreff is a free, German-language Skool community with 574 members positioned explicitly as an options trading group "für Frauen von Frauen," for women, by women.

## What's Actually Inside

The description speaks directly to a specific gap: women who've finished an options trading education program (Ausbildung) and then find themselves without an ongoing peer group to continue the conversation. Optionentreff positions itself as that missing piece, offering Gleichgesinnte, like-minded peers, for exchange and regelmäßige Live-Calls, regular live calls. That suggests the core value is less about teaching options trading from scratch and more about sustaining momentum and community after initial training elsewhere.

Because the description assumes members already have some foundational education, this looks like a continuation or practice community rather than a beginner's course, though it doesn't specify prerequisites or a minimum experience level required to join.

That positioning also suggests the group is meant to fill a social gap as much as an educational one, giving members somewhere to keep discussing trades and strategy after a formal program ends rather than being left to figure things out alone.

## Pros

- Free to join, giving women who've already trained in options trading a no-cost way to stay connected with peers
- Regular live calls provide a concrete, recurring touchpoint rather than a passive forum
- Clear "for women, by women" positioning may create a more comfortable space for women in a trading niche that skews heavily male elsewhere

## Cons

- No independent reviews found beyond the Skool listing, so there's no outside confirmation of call quality, attendance, or trading outcomes discussed
- Options trading carries real financial risk, and nothing in the description suggests this community offers licensed financial advice rather than peer discussion
- The description assumes prior training, so complete beginners to options trading may find the group less suited to their starting point

## Who It's a Good Fit For

German-speaking women who have already completed options trading education elsewhere and want an ongoing, free peer community with regular live calls to keep practicing and discussing strategy.

## Who Should Skip It

Complete beginners to options trading should look for foundational education first, since this community appears built for post-training continuation rather than teaching options from zero. Non-German speakers will also find limited value given the language focus, and anyone should treat trading discussion here as peer input, not licensed financial advice.
