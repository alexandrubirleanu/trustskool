# AI SEO Mastery with Caleb Ulku Review 2026: Is It Worth $27/Month?

AI SEO Mastery is a 3,225-member Skool community for freelancers and agency owners who want to fold AI-era search tactics into an SEO service business. It's run by Caleb Ulku and priced at $27/month, with a 7-day risk-free trial and a money-back window if you cancel within the first week ([Skool](https://www.skool.com/ai-seo-mastery/about)) — a real trial period, which is unusual and a genuine point in its favor compared to communities with no-refund policies.

## What's actually inside

The community centers on a continually updated SEO curriculum rather than a one-time recorded course, plus ready-to-use AI prompts and standard operating procedures the about page says are pulled directly from Caleb's own agency. Monthly live "hot-seat" calls work through real member problems: site technical issues, client proposals, and pricing objections, not just generic strategy. The specific hook of the community is positioning for AI-driven search: ranking in ChatGPT and other AI answer engines and traditional Google results simultaneously, alongside more conventional local map-pack and organic ranking work. Members get access to a private network of other freelancers and agency owners built around the same material.

## Who runs it

Caleb Ulku is described on the community page as the owner of a seven-figure SEO agency and as having been featured in Forbes ([Skool](https://www.skool.com/ai-seo-mastery/about)). He maintains an active YouTube channel and a personal site (calebulku.com) built around the same AI SEO Mastery brand and a related "Pro" course, which shows a consistent, sustained public presence rather than a one-off Skool listing ([YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@calebulku), [calebulku.com](https://calebulku.com/)). That consistency across platforms is a reasonable, if not fully independently audited, signal that this is a real, ongoing agency operator rather than a course-only persona.

## Pros

- A genuine 7-day trial with a money-back option is a real risk-reducer that most communities in this price bracket don't offer.
- Content is explicitly framed as "AI-era SEO" rather than static, years-old ranking tactics, which matters in a field where Google's and AI search engines' behavior keeps shifting.
- Monthly live hot-seat calls addressing real member situations (not just canned Q&A) is a concrete, checkable deliverable rather than a vague promise of "community support."
- $27/month is low relative to the other communities in this niche, lowering the cost of testing whether it's a fit.

## Cons

- Specific income claims on the page ("$5k+/mo retainers... in weeks") follow the same aggressive pattern common across Skool marketing copy and should be treated as illustrative rather than typical or guaranteed.
- As with most single-operator communities, the depth of ongoing support scales with how much time Caleb personally has for hot-seat calls as membership grows past 3,000+; it's worth checking current call cadence and turnout before joining.
- The Forbes feature and "7-figure agency" framing come from the community's own page; we found supporting public presence (YouTube, a standalone site) but not independent third-party verification of the specific revenue figure.

## Who it's a good fit for

Freelancers and small agency owners who already do SEO work and want a lower-cost way to stay current on how AI search is changing client deliverables, without committing to Caleb's more expensive standalone "Pro" course, are the clearest fit. The live hot-seat format suits people who want feedback on an actual client situation rather than generic theory.

## Who should skip it

Total SEO beginners with no existing clients may get less immediate value from a community built around live troubleshooting of active client work rather than foundational teaching. If you need structured, ground-up SEO fundamentals first, a dedicated beginner course may serve you better before this community.
