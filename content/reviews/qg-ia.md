At $79 a month, the pricing sits meaningfully above free or low-cost communities in this space, which typically signals a more hands-on format, whether that's more frequent live sessions, closer access to Ludo Salenne directly, or a more developed library of methods. - Being run and described directly by Ludo Salenne gives the community a clear point of accountability rather than an anonymous or faceless operation.

## Cons
