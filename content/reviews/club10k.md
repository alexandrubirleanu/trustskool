# Club 10K: Expertise & IA Review 2026: What $29/Month Actually Includes

Club 10K: Expertise & IA is a Spanish-language Skool community with 518 members, priced at $29 a month. The description is unusually itemized for a Skool listing: the goal is earning over $3,000 a month using AI to sell an existing skill or expertise, and membership includes a GoHighLevel (GHL) subaccount, more than 30 courses, done-for-you ("copy-paste") funnels, and weekly coaching.

## What's Actually Inside

What stands out is the specificity of the included deliverables rather than vague promises. A GHL subaccount is a concrete software tool (used for CRM, funnels, and automation), and bundling that into a $29/month membership is notable since GoHighLevel subaccounts often carry separate costs elsewhere. Combined with 30+ courses and ready-made funnel templates, the offer positions itself as a more complete toolkit than a typical content-only community, on top of the weekly live coaching component.

The overall model, package an existing skill, use AI to help deliver or market it, and sell access through funnels built on a CRM subaccount, follows a fairly common structure in the online course and coaching space right now. What differentiates one version of this model from another is usually execution quality and support, which the itemized inclusions here at least attempt to make concrete rather than leaving to imagination.

## Pros

- Itemized inclusions (GHL subaccount, 30+ courses, funnel templates, weekly coaching) rather than a vague content promise, giving a clearer sense of what $29/month actually buys
- Bundling a CRM/funnel tool subaccount into the price could represent real added value compared to communities that only offer video lessons
- Weekly coaching adds a live, recurring touchpoint on top of the self-paced course library

## Cons

- No independent reviews were found beyond the Skool listing, so it's unclear whether the GHL subaccount and funnel templates hold up in practice or if there are usage limits not mentioned
- The $3K/month income figure is presented as a goal or promise in the marketing, not a guaranteed or independently verified outcome for members

## Who It's a Good Fit For

Spanish-speaking professionals who already have marketable expertise and want a bundled toolkit (software, courses, templates, coaching) to package and sell it with AI, rather than building each piece separately.

## Who Should Skip It

Anyone without an existing skill or expertise to sell, or those who prefer negotiating their own software tools rather than working within a bundled subaccount.
