# The Next Level Review 2026: Free Israeli Business Growth Club on Skool

The Next Level is a free, 475-member Hebrew-language Skool community that describes itself, in its own words, as the leading business club in Israel, aimed at launching entrepreneurs to their next level using what it calls proven growth methods.

## What's Actually Inside

The description centers on two ideas: proven growth methods for entrepreneurs, and revealing how any expert can build a profitable digital business earning income around the clock from anywhere. That's a broad promise spanning business growth strategy and a specific business model, expert-led digital business, rather than a single narrow skill or tool.

The "any expert can build a digital business" framing is a common structure in business communities worldwide, the idea being that existing knowledge or experience can be packaged into courses, coaching, or content and sold online. What's harder to assess from the description alone is how much of the community's content is generic business motivation versus concrete, step-by-step packaging and marketing guidance, since both tend to get described using similar language.

For Hebrew-speaking entrepreneurs specifically, finding a business community built for their own market and language, rather than a translated or adapted English-language program, is itself a meaningful advantage, independent of how the more superlative marketing claims eventually hold up once inside.

## Pros

- Free to join, removing financial risk for entrepreneurs curious about the content.
- Positioned specifically for the Israeli market and Hebrew-speaking entrepreneurs, which may be harder to find than English-language business communities.
- The framing toward experts building their own digital business gives a fairly specific target audience rather than a completely generic "grow your business" pitch.

## Cons

- "Leading business club in Israel" is a self-applied label in the community's own description, not an independently verified ranking or accreditation.
- The 24/7-income-from-anywhere framing is a broad claim common across business and digital-income communities, and the description doesn't detail specific methods, case studies, or numbers.
- No independent reviews of this community were found beyond its own Skool listing.

## Who It's a Good Fit For

Hebrew-speaking experts and entrepreneurs who want to build a digital business around their expertise and are comfortable evaluating bold marketing language against the actual content once inside.

## Who Should Skip It

Anyone expecting a specific, narrow curriculum or independently verified outcome data before joining should ask the community directly, since the listing leans on broad claims rather than concrete specifics.
