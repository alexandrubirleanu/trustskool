## What's Actually Inside

That positioning fits a moment many small businesses are now in: after an initial wave of AI experimentation, plenty of owners have tried several tools without a coherent plan for how they fit together operationally. A community built around "method" and "clear choices" is implicitly speaking to that frustration, offering a filtering and decision framework rather than yet another list of tools to try.
