# PILAmisu Review 2026: Free Taiwan Pilates Instructor Community

PILAmisu (皮拉米蘇) is a free Skool community with 534 members, written in Chinese and built specifically for Pilates instructors. According to the community's own description, it was created to support Pilates teachers, with the founder currently teaching Pilates in Taipei, Taiwan.

## What's Actually Inside

Based on the description, the community exists as a peer network where Pilates instructors can exchange experience with each other, rather than a formal certification or training program. The listing explicitly welcomes people who aren't Pilates instructors themselves but want to understand the profession better, which suggests the group also functions as an informal window into the trade for the curious, alongside serving working instructors.

## Pros

- Free to join, which lowers the barrier for Pilates instructors and curious outsiders alike
- Specific professional niche (Pilates teaching) rather than general fitness content
- Explicitly open to non-instructors who want to learn about the profession, widening who can get value from it

## Cons

- The description doesn't detail any structured curriculum, certification path, or teaching materials, so it reads more as a discussion space than a training program
- No independent reviews were found beyond the platform listing, and content is in Chinese, which limits accessibility for non-Chinese speakers evaluating it

## Who It's a Good Fit For

Pilates instructors, particularly those based in or connected to Taiwan, who want to trade notes and experience with peers in their own language, plus anyone curious about the Pilates teaching profession.

## Who Should Skip It

Non-Chinese speakers, or instructors looking for a formal certification program rather than a peer discussion community, should look elsewhere.
