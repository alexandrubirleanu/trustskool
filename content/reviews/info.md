# Info Operator Review 2026: Free Community for Scaling Info Products?

Info Operator is a free, German-language Skool community with 393 members. Per its own description, the model is partnership-based: working alongside content creators to help scale their info-product, meaning a digital course or knowledge product, revenue into six figures.

## What's Actually Inside

The description frames the core activity as partnering with creators ("Partner mit Creatorn") rather than teaching a generic course-creation curriculum to a mass audience. The stated outcome, scaling an info product to six-figure revenue, points toward operational and growth support, things like offer structure, sales systems, or funnel optimization, for creators who already have some form of info product, rather than helping someone build a first product from zero.

Revenue-share or partnership-based models in the info-product space typically mean the community's own incentives are tied directly to a creator's success, rather than a flat course fee paid regardless of outcome, which can align interests well but also means the specific terms, what percentage, what services are actually provided in exchange, matter far more here than in a standard paid-course community. Since the description doesn't spell out those terms, getting them in writing before any partnership begins is the sensible first step.

## Pros

- Free to join, removing cost as a barrier to entry.
- Partnership framing suggests a more hands-on, collaborative relationship with creators rather than one-to-many course delivery alone.
- Specific revenue target of six figures gives a concrete benchmark for what the community is oriented toward achieving.

## Cons

- The description doesn't detail what "partnering" concretely involves, such as revenue share, service delivery, or advisory work, which matters a lot for creators evaluating fit.
- No independent reviews of this community were found beyond its own Skool listing.
- The description doesn't specify whether the partnership involves a revenue share, a flat fee, or another arrangement, which is essential to clarify before agreeing to work together.

## Who It's a Good Fit For

German-speaking content creators who already have an info product and want help scaling its revenue through a more hands-on partnership model.

## Who Should Skip It

Creators without an existing product, who are still at the idea stage, are likely better served by a course-creation-from-scratch community rather than this scaling-focused one, and should firm up their offer before any revenue-sharing arrangement begins.
