# Edinburgh Insiders Review 2026: Is This Free Community Legit?

Edinburgh Insiders is a free Skool community with 681 members, built for one city and one purpose: planning an Edinburgh trip like an insider. The description promises impartial advice from locals and fellow travellers, alongside space to celebrate the city and share memories from past visits.

## What's Actually Inside

The word "impartial" in the description is worth noting on its own, it distinguishes this from tourism-board content or commercial travel guides that push specific paid tours or partner businesses. Instead, the community is framed around genuine local and peer input, mixing two member types explicitly: locals who live in Edinburgh and fellow travellers who've already visited. That combination suggests a mix of practical, current, ground-level tips (locals) and shared traveller experience (past visitors), which together could cover both logistics (where to stay, what to skip) and atmosphere (what a first-time visitor should expect emotionally from the city).

## Pros

- Free to join, making it a no-risk way to gather planning advice before or during an Edinburgh trip.
- The explicit "impartial" framing suggests advice isn't tied to sponsored tours or paid partnerships, which is a real differentiator from many commercial travel content sources.
- Combining local residents and past travellers in one space means questions can get answered from both an insider and a recent-visitor perspective.

## Cons

- We didn't find independent reviews of this specific community beyond its own Skool listing, so we can't verify how active or responsive the community actually is day to day.
- The description doesn't mention any structured itinerary templates or guides, so members likely need to ask specific questions rather than find a pre-built trip plan waiting for them.

## Who It's a Good Fit For

Anyone actively planning a trip to Edinburgh who wants genuine, non-commercial advice from people who actually live there or have recently visited is the clearest fit. Travellers who enjoy sharing their own trip memories and connecting with other Edinburgh enthusiasts, not just extracting information, will likely enjoy the community aspect too.

## Who Should Skip It

Travellers planning trips to other Scottish or UK destinations should look for a more relevant, location-specific community, since this one is focused exclusively on Edinburgh. People wanting a fully packaged, pre-built itinerary rather than a Q&A-style advice community may also find the format less useful than a dedicated guidebook.
