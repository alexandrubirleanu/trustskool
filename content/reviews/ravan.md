The $8,000/month figure quoted in the description is the community's own marketing claim, not something we can independently verify, and should be treated accordingly.

## Cons

- The "$8,000 per month" income claim comes directly from the community's own description and should be read as an aspirational marketing figure, not a typical or guaranteed outcome.
- We found no independent reviews of this community beyond its own Skool listing, and reselling white-label software carries its own separate considerations, ongoing support obligations, platform reliability, and underlying vendor terms, worth researching carefully before committing to sell under your own brand.

## Who It's a Good Fit For

People interested in entering the AI-agent reselling business model without building the underlying technology themselves, and who are comfortable evaluating a stated income claim critically before joining.

## Who Should Skip It

Anyone wanting to learn AI skills for personal or in-house use rather than running a reselling business, or those wary of communities built around specific stated income claims.
