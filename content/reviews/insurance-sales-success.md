Four pillars show up directly in the description: sales, marketing, lead generation, and agency growth. ## Cons

- $97 a month is a real recurring cost, and the description doesn't spell out what's delivered weekly or monthly to justify it.
- The "#1 Community For Insurance Agents" claim is self-described positioning, not an independently verified ranking.
- The description doesn't specify which insurance lines, life, health, Medicare, or property and casualty, the training is built around, which matters since tactics can differ by line of business.

## Who It's a Good Fit For

Insurance agents and agency owners who want to grow past their first sales and are looking for lead generation and agency-building tactics specific to the insurance industry, and who are comfortable with a monthly subscription cost.
