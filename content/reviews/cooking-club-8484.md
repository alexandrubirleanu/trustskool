That's a genuinely useful, specific angle for anyone who's cooked from a different country's cuisine while living somewhere with a different grocery landscape.

## Who Runs It

No owner name is attached to this community in the public data, and it reads more like a grassroots community project than a founder-led brand, consistent with its own framing as a "pure exchange" group rather than a taught curriculum.

## Cons
