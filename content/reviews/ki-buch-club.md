## What's Actually Inside

The description points to AI-assisted writing as the practical core, likely covering how to use AI tools to draft, edit, or structure written work more efficiently. What stands out is the explicit tone-setting: heart and humor alongside the technical AI angle, plus the campfire framing, which suggests this is built to feel like a relaxed, ongoing hangout for writers rather than a formal course delivery.
