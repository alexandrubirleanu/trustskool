# MM Elite Review 2026: A Free Skool Community With an Agency Bench Built In?

## What's Actually Inside

The description lists five distinct service areas rather than one narrow topic: offer and strategy work, Shopify store optimization, email marketing, paid advertising, and graphic design. That breadth suggests the community is aimed at ecommerce operators who need multiple specialist skill sets rather than a single tactic, essentially bundling what would normally be several separate freelancers or agencies into one community. What the description doesn't clarify is whether "access" means live consulting, templates and courses, or simply a network of practitioners to ask questions of.
