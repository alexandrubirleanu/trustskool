# CoderCo DevOps Academy Review 2026: Is $199/Month Worth It to Get Hired?

CoderCo DevOps Academy makes an explicit promise in its tagline: "From Zero to DevOps Pro." At $199 a month and with 981 members, it's positioned as a career-transition program rather than a casual learning community, combining what the description calls "real labs, live sessions and expert support" around the goal of getting hired.

## What's Actually Inside

The three components named in the description each address a different part of learning DevOps: real labs suggest hands-on infrastructure practice rather than passive video watching, live sessions imply scheduled, real-time instruction rather than a static course library, and expert support points to some form of mentorship or Q&A access. Combined with the "get hired" framing, this reads as a bootcamp-style community aimed at people trying to break into DevOps roles rather than DevOps professionals looking for advanced niche content.

## Pros

- The combination of labs, live sessions, and support addresses three different learning needs (practice, instruction, and troubleshooting help) rather than just one.
- Explicit focus on getting hired, not just learning concepts, signals the program is oriented toward career outcomes.
- At 981 members, there's a large enough peer group to make live sessions and community support feel active rather than sparse.

## Cons

- $199/month is a meaningful recurring cost, especially for someone between jobs, and it's worth confirming exactly how many labs, sessions, and support hours are included before committing to several months.
- No owner or instructor credentials are listed in the community's record, and no independent reviews were found beyond the platform listing, so the "get hired" outcome claim should be treated as the community's own framing rather than a verified statistic.

## Who It's a Good Fit For

Career changers or IT professionals actively trying to move into DevOps roles who want structured labs and live instruction rather than a self-paced video course.

## Who Should Skip It

Already-employed senior DevOps engineers looking for advanced or niche content, and budget-conscious beginners who just want to explore DevOps casually before committing to a recurring $199/month cost.
