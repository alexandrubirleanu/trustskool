# Gastro Growth Hub Review 2026: Free Community for Restaurant Owners?

Gastro Growth Hub is a free, German-language Skool community with 397 members built for restaurant owners and operators. The pitch centers on learning directly from Ellis, who according to the community's own description runs three restaurants, employs around 100 staff, and generates roughly 5 million euros in revenue, with an explicit emphasis on practical operating knowledge over theory.

## What's Actually Inside

The description frames the content around three concrete operating pillars: cost calculation (Kalkulation), team leadership, and expansion. For an industry where thin margins and staffing are the two things that sink most operators, that's a focused set of topics rather than a generic "grow your business" pitch. The "Praxis statt Theorie" framing, practice instead of theory, suggests the content leans toward operational specifics like pricing and margin math rather than abstract business strategy.

That practical framing matters in an industry where generic business advice, focus on your brand, delight your customers, often glosses over the unglamorous mechanics of running food service at scale: labor cost percentages, menu engineering, and the operational strain of opening additional locations. A free community built around a single operator's specific numbers gives members a concrete reference point rather than industry-wide averages pulled from a textbook.

## Pros

- Free to join, which lowers the barrier for restaurant owners who are often cash-constrained.
- Content is anchored to a named operator with a specific, stated scale (3 restaurants, roughly 100 staff, roughly 5 million euros in revenue) rather than vague expertise claims.
- Narrow topic focus, cost calculation, team leadership, and expansion, matches the real pain points of restaurant ownership rather than generic entrepreneurship content.

## Cons

## Who It's a Good Fit For

## Who Should Skip It
