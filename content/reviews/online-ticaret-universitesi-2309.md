# Online Ticaret Üniversitesi Review 2026: Free Turkish Business Growth Community

Online Ticaret Üniversitesi, which translates to "Online Commerce University," has built a free Skool community of 16,275 members around business growth for a Turkish-speaking audience. The name promises something academic and structured, though the description itself reads more like a broad promise than a syllabus.

## What's Actually Inside

Translated, the community's description says it's "a development center where you'll find the right knowledge, the right people, and the right opportunities to grow your business." That's a three-part pitch: information, networking, and opportunity, aimed generally at people trying to grow a business rather than at any single niche like e-commerce, dropshipping, or a specific platform.

The "university" branding in the name suggests some kind of curriculum or lesson structure, but the description itself doesn't confirm that directly. It's worth going in aware that the actual content, whether it's live coaching, recorded lessons, or open discussion threads, isn't specified anywhere in what's available here.

## Pros

## Cons
