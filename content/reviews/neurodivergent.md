## What's Actually Inside

The "unlearn the BS" language suggests the group actively pushes back against conventional productivity and business norms rather than just adapting them slightly.

Neurodivergent-specific business communities have grown as more entrepreneurs openly discuss how standard advice around rigid schedules, single-tasking, or conventional networking styles can work against certain neurodivergent profiles. A group organized specifically around this shared experience can offer a level of understanding and adapted strategy that a general small-business community, built around neurotypical defaults, typically wouldn't address directly.
