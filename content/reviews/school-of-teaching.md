The Dutch phrase "leraren en lerarenopleiders" (teachers and teacher trainers) is worth pausing on: it means the group pulls in both classroom teachers and the people who train future teachers, so conversations could span everyday classroom problems as well as broader questions about how teaching itself is taught. The word "leeromgeving," a learning environment, suggests an ongoing space members return to rather than a cohort that graduates and disperses. Since there's no price attached, it's reasonable to assume the value here is the peer exchange itself rather than gated courses or paid coaching calls sitting behind a free tier.
