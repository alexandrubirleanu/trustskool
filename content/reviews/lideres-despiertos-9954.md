# Líderes Despiertos Review 2026: A Free Leadership Community With an AI Twist

## What's Actually Inside

The described arc, "del despertar al estándar profesional," from awakening to professional standard, suggests the program treats leadership development as a progression rather than a single skill to pick up, starting with some kind of inner or mindset shift before arriving at professional-level capability. The named AI component, Future Lab, is the more distinctive detail here: describing it as a "gimnasio de liderazgo con IA," an AI leadership gym, implies practice-based, repeated exercises rather than passive lecture content, with AI presumably used as a coaching or simulation tool rather than just a marketing label. The Skool community itself is described as the meeting point and home base for that broader Future Lab initiative. Pairing a leadership philosophy with a named practice tool is a somewhat distinctive move in a crowded leadership-development space, where much of the competing content still relies on lecture-style video and static frameworks rather than interactive practice.
