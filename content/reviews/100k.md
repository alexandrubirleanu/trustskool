# LetsGetFunded Inner Circle Review 2026: Is $63/Month Worth It for Credit and Funding Help?

Worth flagging upfront: the community's own page text advertises "$97/month" as the current price, while the listed price in the data behind this review is $63/month, so it's worth confirming the current price directly before joining.

## What's Actually Inside

Credit and funding are treated as intertwined, the pitch implies that fixing or building credit is a step toward securing business funding, though the description doesn't detail exactly which funding sources, business credit cards, lines of credit, lender relationships, members get help accessing.
