# Spotless - Beauty Power Circle Review 2026: Is This Free Beauty Community Worth Joining?

Spotless - Beauty Power Circle is a free, German-language Skool community with 634 members, but it isn't open to the general public in the way most Skool communities are. Its own description states it's exclusively for participants of the Spotless Academy, positioned as a companion space for exchange, support, live calls, and tips for building a successful beauty business.

## What's Actually Inside

Based on the description, this functions as a members-only extension of a separate paid program, the Spotless Academy, rather than a standalone community someone could join cold. The stated mix of peer exchange, support, and live calls suggests it's designed to keep Academy participants engaged and accountable between formal lessons, with beauty-industry success as the explicit shared goal.

Companion communities tied to a paid course or academy are common on Skool, and they typically work best when the parent program itself delivers the core curriculum while the community handles ongoing motivation and troubleshooting. Prospective Academy participants should treat this circle as one piece of the overall Spotless offering rather than the main deliverable, and confirm with the Academy directly what's covered in the paid program versus what happens here in the free companion space.

## Pros

- Live calls give Academy participants direct, real-time access alongside their core program.
- Peer exchange with other beauty-industry participants going through the same training.
- Free to access for those already enrolled, so it doesn't add extra cost on top of Academy tuition.

## Cons

Because access is described as exclusive to Spotless Academy participants, this isn't something outside businesses can simply join to sample, and the listing doesn't say what happens to a member's access if they finish or leave the Academy program itself. We also found no independent reviews of this specific circle beyond its own Skool listing.

## Who It's a Good Fit For

People already enrolled in, or seriously considering, the Spotless Academy beauty program who want an ongoing peer and live-call support space alongside it.

## Who Should Skip It

Anyone not enrolled in the Spotless Academy, or looking for a general, open-access beauty business community, should look elsewhere since access here appears gated to existing students. People shopping around for beauty-business education in general would be better served starting with the Academy program itself, since this circle exists to support it rather than replace it.
