# Shawn Moore and Vodyssey Empire Club: A 22-Year Real Estate Veteran's Vacation Rental Mastermind

Vodyssey Empire Club is a paid Skool mastermind built around what it calls "Lifestyle Assets," short-term and vacation rental properties, aimed at giving members everything needed to build, grow, and thrive in that specific corner of real estate investing. The person running it, Shawn Moore, didn't start in vacation rentals at all; he spent two decades building a broader real estate career before finding that niche.

## Who is Shawn Moore

Moore's own account of his start is almost old-fashioned: he studied real estate by reading books at his local public library, and earned his first wholesale check right after finishing college ([Vodyssey, About](https://vodyssey.com/about/)). Over the following 22 years, he built experience across home flipping, rental portfolios, land development deals, and luxury resort property sales, according to that same page. His LinkedIn profile lists him as Founder and CEO at Vodyssey, describing his focus as building financial freedom through vacation homes.

His pivot toward vacation rentals specifically dates to 2006, when he bought his first vacation rental property and, in his words, never looked back. From there he built Vodyssey into what his own materials describe as the top short-term rental education company and mastermind group in the space, with a collective portfolio across his community now exceeding 1,000 properties in the US and several international markets, and his team analyzing more than 100 properties a month across those markets.

## What members actually get

Vodyssey Empire Club is structured as an ongoing coaching and mentorship program rather than a single course, with weekly coaching sessions guiding members through acquisition, property management, and marketing for their own vacation rental investments. The community currently runs with five community admins, suggesting an active, staffed support structure rather than a single founder handling every member interaction directly.

Moore's team's monthly analysis of 100-plus properties across different markets is positioned as ongoing market intelligence for members, rather than a one-time training that goes stale as short-term rental markets shift.

## Why it's grown

Moore's 22 years of broader real estate experience, spanning flipping, rentals, land development, and luxury resort sales, before he ever specialized in vacation rentals, gives Vodyssey Empire Club a depth of market cycles and property types most single-niche vacation rental coaches haven't lived through. Having weathered multiple real estate cycles across different asset types before settling on short-term rentals as his specialty adds a layer of credibility that a coach who started and stayed only in vacation rentals wouldn't have.

The scale of his own team's portfolio and ongoing market analysis also functions as proof of execution rather than just teaching theory, which matters in a niche where property-specific due diligence errors can be expensive for new investors to make on their own.
