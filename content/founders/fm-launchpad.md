# Aaron Fletcher and Growthworks Community: The Fletcher Method Behind 24,000 Product Launches

Growthworks Community exists to solve one specific problem: helping people go from a raw idea to a live course or high-ticket offer they can actually launch with confidence. Over 24,000 members have joined the free group, working through a 5-day challenge built around tools and templates rather than open-ended advice.

## Who is Aaron Fletcher

Fletcher is the founder of both the Fletcher Method and Growthworks, and according to his LinkedIn profile, he has helped more than 50,000 coaches and consultants while generating over $30 million in online sales through his frameworks ([LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/fletchmonster/)). He's based in Bend, Oregon, and attended Chapman University before building his career in direct-response marketing for small business owners.

His flagship program, the Fletcher Method CORE training, has reportedly been used by more than 6,500 entrepreneurs and small business owners to build and launch online businesses. He's also the author of "Stand Out," a book aimed at helping small business owners build a simple, effective online marketing plan without relying on jargon or complexity, and Growthworks.io itself operates as what he calls the first done-for-you content engine, pairing a CRM with funnels, email sequences, and scheduling tools built on his own product methodology ([Trustpilot reviews for Growthworks](https://www.trustpilot.com/review/www.growthworks.io)).

## What members actually get

The core of the free community is a 5-day challenge that walks members from an unformed idea to a launch-ready offer, step by step, using the same templates and tools Fletcher built for his paid clients. Members share real progress inside the group, including specific wins with course launches and B2C client results, and the classroom content breaks down messaging and offer construction in granular, module-by-module detail.

The community also functions as a support layer for people already using Growthworks' broader done-for-you systems, giving them a place to troubleshoot and compare notes with others running similar launches.

## Why it's grown

Fletcher's track record predates Skool by years, built through the Fletcher Method's direct-response marketing training and Growthworks' software business. That existing base of coaches and consultants gave Growthworks Community an audience to draw from immediately rather than needing to build one from a standing start.

The community also benefits from specificity: rather than a general "grow your business" pitch, the explicit promise is a working launch within five days, using tools that already exist rather than concepts members have to build themselves. For an audience that has often already tried and stalled out on a course launch once before, that concreteness is a meaningfully different pitch than most launch-strategy communities make.
