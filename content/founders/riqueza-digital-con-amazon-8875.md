# Jainmy Martínez and Digital Income Secrets: Inside Skool's Amazon Income Community

DIGITAL INCOME SECRETS is a free, Spanish-language Skool community with more than 2,600 members, built around a promise familiar to anyone who follows Latin American digital marketing content: generate income through Amazon, surrounded by a team pushing you toward "flying high." Behind the "#ÁguilasDigitales" branding is Jainmy Martínez, a Dominican entrepreneur whose Digital Income Secrets 2.0 launch got enough local attention to be covered by several outlets in the Dominican Republic.

## Who is Jainmy Martínez

Martínez has been building digital businesses and e-commerce brands for more than 15 years, including ventures like MySexyStyles and Miracle Body Nutrition, and describes herself as a self-made entrepreneur with over $6.1 million in online sales ([Listín Diario coverage](https://listindiario.com/las-sociales/20260113/digital-income-secrets-2-0-presenta-credenciales_889650.html)). Dominican press outlets covered the January launch of her "Digital Income Secrets 2.0" platform, describing her as a digital entrepreneur teaching people to build online businesses from a phone, without needing inventory, employees, or an office ([El Nuevo Diario](https://elnuevodiario.com.do/jainmy-martinez-presenta-una-plataforma-para-crear-negocios-digitales-desde-el-celular/); [La Fórmula Radio](https://laformularadio.do/jainmy-martinez-lanza-digital-income-secrets-2-0/); [Hoy Digital](https://hoy.com.do/vivir/rostros/jainmy-martinez-lanza-digital-income-secrets-2-0_1073438.html)).

According to that coverage, the platform combines AI, automation, and the Amazon Influencer Program with what Martínez describes as spiritual and motivational principles, aimed at people who want a real digital business rather than just another course purchase. She frames her mission as restoring hope alongside teaching income generation, a message that runs through both her Skool community and her paid academy.

## What members actually get

Inside the free Skool community, the pitch is straightforward: learn to earn with Amazon while being part of a team that pushes members toward bigger goals, per the community's own description. That typically means guidance on the Amazon Influencer Program, on-site commissions, and affiliate marketing basics, the same pillars Martínez teaches in her paid Digital Income Secrets 2.0 academy, which layers in AI tools for content creation.

The free Skool tier functions as the entry point into that world, a common structure for Spanish-language income communities, where the community itself builds trust and audience before members are invited into paid coaching or academy tiers.

## Why it's grown

Martínez's growth story is tied directly to an existing, recognizable brand in the Dominican Republic rather than cold Skool discovery. Having built and sold products for over 15 years before turning to teaching, she brought a pre-existing reputation and press relationships into her Digital Income Secrets launch, which multiple Dominican outlets picked up within the same news cycle in January. That kind of simultaneous, multi-outlet local coverage is unusual for a Skool community founder and suggests active PR and an existing media profile, not just organic content marketing.

The free community also lowers the barrier to entry for a Spanish-speaking audience that may be wary of paying upfront for an unfamiliar income method, letting the "Águilas Digitales" framing and group identity do the work of building trust before any sales conversation happens.
