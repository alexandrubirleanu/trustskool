# 19Keys and HIGH - LVL CLUB: Inside Skool's Financial Literacy and Consciousness Community

HIGH - LVL CLUB is a Skool community of just over 5,100 members built around the personal brand of 19Keys, promising members they'll "reach their highest capacity, live financially abundant, and master higher consciousness." Unlike most Skool founders, 19Keys arrived with an existing media career rather than building his audience on the platform itself.

## Who is 19Keys

19Keys' legal name is Jibrial Muhammad. He was born in St. Louis and moved to Oakland at age two when his father relocated for work, later splitting time between the two cities as a teenager after his parents divorced ([The Fox Magazine interview](https://thefoxmagazine.com/featured/19keys-talks-key-to-financial-literacy-growing-up-muslim-more/)). He's described Oakland as instilling a "militant consciousness" in him, while St. Louis gave him a rawer, street-level view of a community where, in his own words, many people he grew up around "are dead." He completed about a year of college studying business marketing before dropping out following an assault charge he was ultimately cleared of.

Before building his current media brand, he worked for Prada and ran a clothing store called Musahill in Oakland ([The Fox Magazine](https://thefoxmagazine.com/featured/19keys-talks-key-to-financial-literacy-growing-up-muslim-more/); [Revolt.tv](https://www.revolt.tv/article/2022-12-15/262107/19-keys-says-he-fired-his-boss-in-order-to-become-one)). He's since built the High-Level Conversations podcast, whose episode with researcher Billy Carson has drawn more than 5.5 million views, and hosts a touring live event series across more than a dozen cities called the Highest Lvl Tour ([The Fox Magazine](https://thefoxmagazine.com/featured/19keys-talks-key-to-financial-literacy-growing-up-muslim-more/)). His other stated ventures include SuperMind Coffee, Crownz Society, and Goldewater ([19keys.com](https://www.19keys.com/)). AllHipHop has profiled him as someone positioned to "empower individuals to be the best version of themselves" ([AllHipHop](https://allhiphop.com/breeding-ground/19keys/)).

## What members actually get

The community blends the two pillars 19Keys is best known for across his podcast and public commentary: practical financial literacy content and the self-development, spiritual framing he calls "higher consciousness," touching on identity, discipline, and faith alongside wealth-building. His own site describes the structure around the club as including monthly consultations for goal alignment plus a "High Level University" of master classes, e-books, and behind-the-scenes content, alongside occasional live stops on the Highest Lvl Tour ([19keys.com](https://www.19keys.com/)).

## Why it's grown

19Keys didn't need Skool to build an audience. He'd already logged millions of podcast views, run a multi-city touring event, and earned coverage from outlets like AllHipHop and Revolt well before HIGH - LVL CLUB existed as a Skool page. For a community organized around financial and personal transformation, a founder whose own biography reads as a visible before-and-after, from a clothing store in Oakland to touring more than a dozen cities as a self-described thought leader, functions as the product's core credibility more than any specific curriculum promise in the community's description.
