# Aaron Brewer and Agency World: Inside Skool's 6,400-Member Agency Mastermind

Agency World is a 6,432-member Skool community describing itself as "the #1 agency mastermind in the world," aimed at agency owners who want to grow real, sustainable businesses rather than chase vanity metrics. Current access runs at a nominal $1 [Skool](https://www.skool.com/agencyworld/about).

## Who runs it

Agency World is currently led by Aaron Brewer, who took over leadership of the group in a rebrand back to the "Agency World" name after a period operating as "Staffing World" [Skool](https://www.skool.com/agencyworld/agency-world-takeover). Brewer's own bio states he scaled two real estate marketing agencies to $200,000 per month in revenue, personally closed sales at a 57% close rate, and has mentored dozens of agency owners from zero to six-figure months, in some cases in under 30 days [Skool profile](https://www.skool.com/@aaron-brewer-8076).

Worth being transparent about: this research could not determine who originally founded the community before it changed hands and names. Skool communities that get renamed and handed off between owners are not unusual, but it means "founder" and "current leader" are two different questions here — Brewer is verifiably the person running Agency World today, not necessarily its original creator.

## What members actually get

Agency World positions itself around "scaling agencies with integrity" — live calls, an active community of agency owners across a range of sizes (the group's own materials reference members running seven-, eight-, and nine-figure agencies), and direct access to Brewer for sales and offer feedback. Posts inside the community include live sales reviews, where members bring real calls or pitches for critique, and discussions specific to the operational challenges of running a service agency: hiring, client retention, and pricing.

## Why it's grown

Free and near-free agency masterminds are common on Skool, but most either stay small or drift into low-signal self-promotion once they scale. Agency World's differentiator is Brewer's direct, hands-on coaching style — live sales reviews with real numbers attached (a stated 57% personal close rate) give the community a specific, checkable credibility marker rather than vague "I built a 7-figure agency" claims with no detail. The rebrand back to "Agency World" after the "Staffing World" period also suggests active, ongoing management rather than a community left on autopilot, which matters in a niche where agency-building tactics (platforms, ad costs, hiring models) shift constantly and a stale community loses relevance fast.

Given the ownership change and rename, prospective members should look at recent activity and reviews rather than assume continuity with whatever built the community's original reputation under its earlier ownership.
