# Edgar Phillips and The Neuro-Coach Method: The Free Front Door to a South African Trauma-Healing Practice

The Neuro-Coach Method -Gratis is a free, Afrikaans-titled Skool community with around 4,200 members, positioned as an entry point: "Gratis inligting oor Neuro-Coaching en wat dit behels, sodat jy 'n ingeligte besluit kan neem" (free information about Neuro-Coaching and what it involves, so you can make an informed decision). It's the awareness layer sitting in front of a much older, more established training practice.

## Who is Edgar Phillips

Edgar Phillips is credited directly as the community's founder on its Skool About page, and he's the developer and trainer behind Multi-Level Neuro-Processing (MLNP) and The Neuro-Coach Method, a brain-body facilitation approach used in both therapeutic and coaching contexts ([Institute for Multi-Level Neuro-Processing](https://www.multilevelprocessing.com/business-directory-2/edgar-s-phillips-2/)). He's based in Alberton, South Africa, and his credentials span multiple modalities: he became an Imago Professional Facilitator in 2008, earned a postgraduate diploma in Functional Therapy in 2010, was certified as an EFT (Emotional Freedom Techniques) practitioner the same year, and became a registered International Master Brainspotting Therapist in 2015 ([ICR Coach Register listing](https://icr-coachregister.com/organizations/the-neuro-coach-method-edgar-phillips)).

His stated professional focus is helping people heal from the effects of trauma, with a particular specialization in performance anxiety and repetitive sports performance problems, work he's applied with athletes looking to break through performance plateaus.

## What members actually get

Because this specific community is explicitly the free, informational tier, "Gratis" is in the name, it isn't where the deeper MLNP or Neuro-Coach Method training happens. Instead it functions as an on-ramp: a place where people curious about Neuro-Coaching can learn what the method actually involves, ask questions, and decide whether to move toward the fuller training and certification Phillips offers through the Institute for Multi-Level Neuro-Processing.

That structure, a free explainer community feeding a paid certification and coaching practice, is a common shape for practitioner-led education on Skool, and it fits neatly with Phillips's long-standing, credential-heavy training organization that predates his presence on the platform.

## Why it's grown

The community's growth is best explained by demand for accessible information about a niche method with real therapeutic roots. MLNP and brainspotting are specialized modalities that most people have never encountered, and a free space to ask basic questions before committing to paid coach training lowers the barrier substantially. For a trauma-focused practice built over more than fifteen years of accumulated certifications, a free Skool tier gives Phillips a scalable way to qualify and educate prospective coaches and clients before they ever book a paid session or enroll in certification.
