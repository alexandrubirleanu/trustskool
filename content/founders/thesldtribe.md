# Dr. Sonia Lewis and The SLD Tribe: Inside Skool's 6,300-Member Student Loan Community

The SLD Tribe is a free Skool community of about 6,321 members focused specifically on student loan clarity, offering education on rules and options rather than one-on-one financial advice. It's the public arm of an existing student loan consulting practice run by Dr. Sonia Lewis.

## Who is Dr. Sonia Lewis

Dr. Sonia Lewis is known publicly as "the Student Loan Doctor," a brand she runs across social media and her own education platform. Her Instagram account, @thestudentloandoctor, positions her as an expert specifically on student loan debt ([Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/thestudentloandoctor/)). She also operates SLD University, a paid education program on student loan repayment and forgiveness strategy, with its own dedicated site ([SLD University](https://www.slduniversity.org/)) and a course listing on Teachable under her Student Loan Doctor brand ([Teachable listing](https://sld-university.teachable.com/p/the-student-loan-doctor-university)).

The SLD Tribe on Skool is described as the public education and community space powered by her broader Student Loan Doctor brand, and her stated track record, having helped more than 55,000 borrowers and facilitated over $65 million in student loan forgiveness, is the credential the whole community is positioned around.

## What members actually get

The Skool community is free to join and is explicitly framed as general education rather than personalized loan strategy: members get informed about the rules governing student loan forgiveness, repayment plans, and related programs so they can make more confident decisions about their own situation. It's a deliberately public, lower-commitment layer that sits in front of Dr. Lewis's paid offerings, like SLD University, for people who want individualized planning.

That distinction matters in the student loan space specifically, where a lot of advice online is either overly generic or, worse, comes from people incentivized to push a specific loan servicing product. Positioning The SLD Tribe as general education, with paid, personalized strategy work available separately, is a clearer boundary than most communities in this niche draw.

## Why it's grown

Student loan forgiveness rules have shifted considerably and confusingly in recent years, and Dr. Lewis's stated track record, tens of thousands of borrowers helped and tens of millions in loans forgiven, gives The SLD Tribe a credibility anchor that resonates with an audience that's often been burned by unclear or contradictory guidance elsewhere. At 6,321 members with no cost to join, the free community model lowers the barrier for borrowers who are anxious about debt and unlikely to pay for advice before they trust the source.

The "Doctor" branding and years of public content under a single consistent identity also help here: unlike anonymous forums full of conflicting anecdotes about loan forgiveness, members are following one named, credentialed source with a documented history in the space.
