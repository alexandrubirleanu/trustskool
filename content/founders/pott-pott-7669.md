# Dr. Oliver Pott and POTT & POTT: A SPIEGEL Bestselling Professor's Skool Community

POTT & POTT is a free, German-language Skool community with more than 2,400 members, framed around energy, consciousness, and "shapers of the new era" learning to unfold their own creative power. Behind the somewhat esoteric branding sits an unusually credentialed founder: Dr. Oliver Pott, a longtime entrepreneurship professor with a track record of building and selling real companies before he ever built a community business.

## Who is Dr. Oliver Pott

Pott has served as a professor of entrepreneurship and internet marketing at the University of Paderborn since 2010, a role his own materials describe him holding for 14 years running ([founder.de](https://founder.de/buchen/)). Alongside that academic post, he has founded multiple internet businesses, including the software platform Blitzbox, which he sold to a publicly listed company for several million euros within a short period, according to his own account. Since 2005, he has run founder.de, a platform teaching people how to launch digital business models without startup capital.

His books have repeatedly landed on major German bestseller lists, including SPIEGEL, Handelsblatt, Manager-Magazin, BILD, Amazon, and Thalia, with his book on community-building, "Wissen zu Geld," specifically achieving SPIEGEL bestseller status. Forbes has separately described him as one of the most successful internet entrepreneurs in the German-speaking world. On top of the academic and publishing credentials, Pott is listed as a Skool Games winner, Skool's own competitive recognition for top-performing community builders on the platform, giving him a direct, platform-verified track record on Skool itself rather than just an outside reputation. He runs POTT & POTT alongside Monika Pott.

## What members actually get

POTT & POTT's own framing centers on energetic tools and frequency work aimed at helping members consciously direct their creative power and lead their environment "with heart and consciousness," language that blends business-building content with a more spiritual, consciousness-focused approach. A related paid tier, the Community MasterClass, runs at roughly 99 euros a month, suggesting the free Skool community functions as an entry point into Pott's broader paid coaching ecosystem around the same themes of community-building and personal transformation.

Given Pott's academic focus on entrepreneurship, the practical spine underneath the more spiritual language likely includes real community-monetization and business-building frameworks drawn from his own 20-plus years founding and scaling companies.
