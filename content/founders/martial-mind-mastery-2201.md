# Sifu Paul Resnick and Martial Mind Mastery: A Wing Chun Master's Skool Community

Martial Mind Mastery is a $49-a-month Skool community with over 2,500 members centered on what its founder calls a "martial mindset" applied to everyday life. Unlike most fitness-adjacent Skool communities, this one is run by someone with an unusually deep martial arts resume: Sifu Paul Resnick, a 5th degree Wing Chun Kung Fu black belt who has spent more than two decades running training programs out of Amsterdam.

## Who is Sifu Paul Resnick

Resnick's own site lays out a career built on two parallel tracks: martial arts and business ([About Me, martialmindmastery.com](https://martialmindmastery.com/about-me/)). He holds a 5th degree black belt in Wing Chun Kung Fu, teaches Tai Chi, and has more than 40 years of martial arts training behind him. Since 2003, he has served as International Program Director at IWKA in Amsterdam, a role he has held for over two decades.

Before that, according to his bio, he owned and managed a market research company in Amsterdam with more than 40 employees, giving him a business background that predates his current coaching work. His site also describes a difficult stretch in his late thirties, a divorce, the loss of a close friend to cancer, and a separate relationship tragedy, which he says he channeled into developing what he calls The Ultimate Quest, the transformational methodology underpinning his coaching. He's now a certified Transformational Guide with more than 25 years of coaching experience, and describes having taught and guided thousands of students, clients, and professionals over that period, including people from government agencies, police academies, financial institutions, and professional sports.

## What members actually get

Inside the paid Skool community, Resnick's stated approach blends internal martial arts principles, specifically the philosophy underlying Wing Chun and Tai Chi, with psychology, neuroscience, and Buddhist and Taoist concepts, aimed at helping members build self-mastery rather than just physical technique. That's a notably different pitch from most "martial arts mindset" content online, which tends to stay at the level of motivational quotes rather than a structured internal-arts framework taught by someone who has actually run a martial arts program for 20-plus years.

Given the $49 monthly price point and the community's framing around "martial mind set living," members are likely getting structured coaching content, direct access to Resnick, and a peer group working through the same self-mastery framework, positioned as a step above free motivational content but still accessible relative to his 1:1 coaching rates.

## Why it's grown

What separates Martial Mind Mastery from the crowded field of mindset and self-improvement communities on Skool is the sheer specificity and length of Resnick's underlying credentials: a 5th degree black belt earned over four decades, two-plus decades directing an actual martial arts organization, and a prior full career running a mid-sized company before he ever positioned himself as a coach. That combination of technical martial arts authority and real business management experience is unusual, and it gives his mindset content a different texture than most Skool coaches selling discipline frameworks with a much thinner track record behind them.

His willingness to publicly discuss the personal crises that shaped his methodology also adds a layer of credibility that pure "listen to my system" marketing usually lacks.
