# Terrell Gentry and Claude for Founders: Inside Skool's 10,000-Member Claude AI Community

Claude for Founders is a free Skool community, currently just over 10,000 members, built specifically around using Anthropic's Claude and Claude Code to build and sell AI agents. It's run by Terrell Gentry, a self-taught developer who describes himself as an AI founder, developer, and growth hacker, and who has built the community as an extension of a broader no-code AI agent education business.

## Who is Terrell Gentry

Terrell Gentry studied Computer Science at Santiago Canyon College and describes a largely self-taught technical background, having been writing code from a young age, combined with earlier experience in digital marketing and growth strategy work across multiple brands ([CodeMentor profile](https://www.codementor.io/@terrellgentry)). Before Claude for Founders, he built "Build AI Agents Without Coding," a Skool-hosted training program teaching non-programmers to build real-world AI agents using Claude paired with no-code automation tools like n8n, Make.com, Zapier, and Trigger.dev — the same program that appears to have evolved into, or fed into, the Claude for Founders community ([course listing](https://udcourse.com/product/build-ai-agents-without-coding-terrell-gentry/); [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/terrell-gentry)).

Gentry's LinkedIn describes him directly as "Founder @ Claude Community (10k+ members)," and he has publicly discussed related ventures, including a "Voice AI Accelerator" program for building sellable voice AI agents ([LinkedIn post](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/terrell-gentry_finally-im-publicly-opening-the-voice-ai-activity-7392221770730336257-Gz0b)).

## What members actually get

The community's core offering is the Claude Masterclass, focused on properly setting up and running Claude Code rather than using it in an ad hoc way, plus a library of more than 50 copy-paste Claude Code "skills" that don't require writing code, and additional masterclasses on building agents, including voice agents members can package and sell to clients. Gentry's own materials claim the community has generated more than $10 million in combined revenue for member companies through AI agent work, and highlight specific member outcomes — a 14-year-old member reaching $9,100 a month, another member hitting $20,000 a month within 30 days, and a third reaching more than $50,000 a month over a six-month period. These are the community's own self-reported success stories rather than independently audited figures, and should be read as case studies the program chooses to showcase rather than typical or guaranteed outcomes.

Pricing is currently free, with the community's own materials stating price will rise to $40/month once membership hits 10,500 — putting Claude for Founders right at that threshold as of the most recent count.

## Why it's grown

Claude for Founders sits inside one of the fastest-growing corners of the AI education market: communities specifically built around Claude Code, rather than general "AI automation" content, exploded in number through 2025 and 2026 as Claude Code itself became a widely adopted tool for both developers and non-developers building software and automations. Gentry's specific angle, no-code AI agent building rather than traditional software engineering, widens the addressable audience to include non-technical founders and marketers who wouldn't otherwise consider themselves capable of building AI agents.

The free-to-$40 pricing ramp, tied to a visible member-count threshold, is the same urgency mechanic used by several of the largest AI communities on Skool, and gives existing free members a concrete incentive to invite others before the price increase locks in.
