# Andy Elliott and Elite Sales Alliance: A Sales Trainer's 22,000-Member Leadership Group

Elite Sales Alliance calls itself Andy Elliott's number one group for sales and leadership, and it's grown to over 22,000 members on the strength of Elliott's existing reputation as a sales trainer, built well before the Skool community launched.

## Who is Andy Elliott

Elliott founded The Elliott Group, a sales training company headquartered in Fountain Hills, Arizona, alongside his wife Jackie, who is credited on the company's own site as building the business with him ([elliott247.com](https://elliott247.com/home)). Elliott's background is in automotive sales training specifically, and The Elliott Group's website describes him as "The World's #1 Sales Trainer & Business Coach," offering one-on-one coaching, dealership-specific training platforms, and a program called Total Re-Creation aimed at sales professionals.

He hosts a podcast, "Andy Elliott's Elite Mindset Motivation and Sales Training," distributed on Spotify and Apple Podcasts, and runs a YouTube channel under his own name where he publishes sales training and motivational content regularly ([Spotify](https://open.spotify.com/show/5byd10LqZPYJEFnpLcxMdj); [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCIP99i3azLdyYRxnUkSLJ7g)). Elite Sales Alliance's own Skool page describes its curriculum as teaching the framework Elliott used to build a nine-figure business and develop what it calls a magnetic company culture, alongside a five-step course framed as life-changing.

## What members actually get

Members get access to more than 100 hours of Elliott's course material and training, along with regularly updated guides and resources. The community structure includes live weekly Q&A sessions over Zoom directly with Elliott, giving members a recurring opportunity to ask questions rather than working through static content alone. The pitch draws heavily on Elliott's own automotive sales background but is framed broadly enough to apply across sales roles and industries.

The community's tone throughout leans intense and high-commitment, consistent with Elliott's public persona as a high-energy motivational figure, rather than a more reserved, academic approach to sales training.

## Why it's grown

Elite Sales Alliance benefits directly from Elliott's pre-existing audience across YouTube and his podcast, both of which have been running and building a following well before this specific Skool community existed. Automotive and B2B sales professionals already familiar with his training style through free video content have a low-friction path into the paid community, since they've already had a chance to evaluate whether his coaching approach resonates before committing.

Having his wife Jackie built into the business as a visible co-founder also reinforces the "built this together" narrative that runs through Elliott's public content, giving the brand a family-business credibility angle that a purely solo sales-guru persona often lacks.
