# Adam Enfroy and AI Income Blueprint: The Blogger Behind One of Skool's Longest-Running Courses

AI Income Blueprint, previously known as Blog Growth Engine, is a Skool community with close to 4,800 members teaching people how to launch, grow, and scale a $10,000-a-month website using AI. It's the community layer for a course originally built by Adam Enfroy, a blogger whose own site went from zero to hundreds of thousands of monthly readers in under two years, with Colin Shipp running much of the day-to-day mastermind support.

## Who is Adam Enfroy

Enfroy started his blog, adamenfroy.com, in 2019 after an eight-year career in digital marketing that included a stint as affiliate manager for BigCommerce. Within roughly 17 months he had grown his blog's traffic from zero to more than 500,000 monthly readers, and Forbes covered his trajectory in a 2020 profile detailing how the then-32-year-old was making over $80,000 a month from the site with no ad spend ([Forbes](https://www.forbes.com/sites/celinnedacosta/2020/09/01/how-this-32-year-old-entrepreneur-makes-over-80k-a-month-blogging/)). His own site's "My Story" page describes crossing $1 million in revenue within two years and says the blog now generates over $200,000 a month, positioning adamenfroy.com as one of the larger software review sites online ([adamenfroy.com/my-story](https://www.adamenfroy.com/my-story)). He also launched a YouTube channel in 2021 that has grown to roughly 200,000 subscribers, and Muck Rack lists him as a contributing writer with a public byline history ([Muck Rack bio](https://muckrack.com/adam-enfroy-1/bio)).

## What members actually get

According to the Skool community's own listing, AI Income Blueprint is built around a step-by-step system for launching and scaling a blog to five figures a month using current AI tools, an update to the original Blog Growth Engine curriculum that predates the recent wave of AI-assisted content workflows. Members get course access, the mastermind community itself, and, per third-party course reviews, weekly live calls where questions get answered directly.

## Why it's grown

Enfroy built his authority the same way most successful course creators on Skool did: by demonstrating the exact result he now teaches, in his case scaling a personal blog into a seven-figure business inside two years, and then packaging that process for other people to follow. Rebranding from Blog Growth Engine to AI Income Blueprint tracks a real shift in how blogging actually works now that AI tools have changed research and drafting workflows, which keeps a multi-year-old course relevant to a 2026 audience rather than stuck teaching a pre-AI playbook.
