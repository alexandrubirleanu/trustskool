# Frank Nillard and Neural Architects: From a Sold AI Startup to Teaching n8n Agents

Neural Architects is a Skool community of roughly 3,900 members aimed at industry professionals and business owners, about 80% of its membership by its own description, who want to implement AI coding agents to automate their work and build software tools. It's built and run by Frank Nillard, credited as founder on the community's About page.

## Who is Frank Nillard

Frank Nillard built and later exited DocuSphere, an AI agent for data analysis he founded in 2023, and he's since gone on to build ConvertChat, an AI sales agent for WhatsApp ([Founder Story: DocuSphere, YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zafeN9gPZdQ); [DocuSphere Facebook page](https://m.facebook.com/DocuSphere/community/)). He carries recognized standing in the AI education space as a Microsoft and DataCamp AI Partner, with an official contributor profile on Microsoft's own Learn platform ([Microsoft Learn profile](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/community/learn-with/frank-nillard/)), and DataCamp has promoted webinars built around his teaching, including one on building AI agents with n8n without writing code ([DataCamp announcement](https://x.com/DataCamp/status/1933601566738493886)).

By his own account he's a former mechanical engineer and tech consultant who led tech teams across three continents before founding his own software startup and development agency, and his teaching reach spans more than 35,000 subscribers and over a million people educated across platforms.

## What members actually get

Neural Architects offers a free, full course on n8n and AI agents, aimed at helping members automate parts of their work and life and build actual software tools rather than just learning AI concepts in the abstract. The community also promises direct access to Nillard through DMs and post comments, plus live progress updates as he builds, giving members some visibility into an active practitioner's real build process rather than only static, pre-recorded lessons.

## Why it's grown

Nillard's credibility here rests on having actually built and exited an AI company rather than only teaching AI theory, which is a meaningful differentiator in a crowded field of AI-education Skool communities where many instructors have never shipped a product of their own. His official partner status with Microsoft and DataCamp adds a layer of third-party validation that's relatively rare in this space, since those relationships typically require some vetting beyond self-promotion. For business owners and professionals trying to figure out whether AI agent automation is worth their time, a free course from someone who has both built and sold a company in the exact space he's teaching, and who large platforms have chosen to partner with, offers a more concrete signal of expertise than most competing free communities can match.
