# Jacob Blank and Wholesaling Real Estate University: The Teenager Who Wholesaled 400 Houses Before 21

Wholesaling Real Estate is a free Skool community with more than 4,100 members, promising a path to a first wholesaling deal within 30 days with no money or experience required. It's built and run by Jacob Blank, who is credited directly as its founder on the community's own About page.

## Who is Jacob Blank

Jacob Blank started wholesaling real estate, the practice of putting a property under contract and then assigning that contract to an investor for a fee, at just 16 years old. By the time he was 21, he had wholesaled more than 400 houses, a track record documented in a feature profile on his path to becoming a millionaire before turning 20 ([Disrupt Magazine](https://disruptmagazine.com/how-jacob-blank-became-a-millionaire-by-the-age-of-19/)). He's built a public teaching presence around that story, including a YouTube channel where he breaks down real seller calls and closes in detail ([Jacob Blank YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/c/jacobblank)).

His age and speed are central to how he markets his teaching: rather than presenting wholesaling as something that requires years of capital or experience to break into, his own results are the proof point that a teenager with no money and no background in real estate can get a deal done.

## What members actually get

Inside the free Wholesaling Real Estate community, members get roughly 20 training videos walking through how to land a first deal step by step and how to scale from there once the basics click. For members who want deeper access, Blank also runs a Private Inner Circle Wholesaling Real Estate Mentorship group, invite-only, which includes weekly hour-plus group mentorship calls covering how to start or scale a wholesaling business, along with access to more than 75 exclusive A-Z training videos and a vault of contracts, scripts, SOPs and templates pulled from a seven-figure wholesaling operation.

That structure, a free foundational course feeding a paid, higher-touch mentorship tier, is a standard shape for real estate education on Skool, and it lets Blank serve total beginners for free while monetizing the more serious, capital-intensive relationships through the Inner Circle.

## Why it's grown

The core of Blank's appeal is that his own story removes the two most common objections beginners raise about wholesaling: that you need money to start, and that you need years of experience or industry connections to close a deal. Having done exactly what he's teaching, starting at 16 with nothing and reaching 400-plus deals before 21, gives his free community a credibility that's hard to manufacture through marketing copy alone. Combined with public, unscripted content like recorded seller calls, that track record has let the community grow into one of the larger free wholesaling hubs on the platform without relying purely on paid advertising to drive membership.
