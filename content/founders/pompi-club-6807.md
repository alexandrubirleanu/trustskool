# Gerardo Marrero and Método CLEAN: The Nutritionist Behind Skool's Free Glute-Training Community

Método CLEAN is a free, Spanish-language Skool community that has grown to nearly 3,400 members around a specific pitch aimed at women: reducing belly fat while building glutes. It's run by Gerardo Marrero, who signs the community's own description "By Gerardo Marrero."

## Who is Gerardo Marrero

Marrero is a nutritionist, known online as "Nutriologo," who runs an Instagram account under the handle soygerardomarrero with more than 700,000 followers, where he posts nutrition and training content aimed at a broad, Spanish-speaking audience ([Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/soygerardomarrero/)). A feature on the Spanish-language outlet La Madriguera traces his path from competitive runner to nutritionist and natural bodybuilder, describing years spent competing in natural bodybuilding while building out his coaching practice, and framing his own history with nutrition and training as central to how he now teaches others ([La Madriguera profile](https://lamadriguera.com.es/gerardo-marrero-de-corredor-a-nutricionista-y-fisicoculturista-natural-el-ejemplo-de-perseverancia-y-bienestar/)).

That combination, a large, verifiable social following under a consistent handle plus an independent feature piece describing his professional background, places him well outside the anonymous "fitness influencer" category common in this niche.

## What members actually get

Método CLEAN is built around four stated pillars: training focused specifically on glute shape and volume, nutrition strategy aimed at reducing abdominal fat, mindset and discipline work to sustain the transformation, and a support community of women working through the same program together. The community also highlights seven common mistakes Marrero says he's observed repeatedly in women during their first month of trying to change their bodies, positioning the free tier as a diagnostic entry point before any paid coaching relationship.

## Why it's grown

Marrero's existing Instagram audience, in the hundreds of thousands, gives Método CLEAN a large pool of potential members who already follow his nutrition and training content before ever encountering the Skool community itself. Free access removes any financial barrier to joining, which matters in a niche, women's body recomposition content, where paid programs are common and trust in a specific coach's credibility is the main thing standing between a follower and a sign-up.

A body-recomposition pitch built specifically around glutes and abdominal fat, rather than generic weight loss, also gives the community a narrower, more relatable promise for the audience Marrero has already built on social media.

The Spanish-language women's fitness space online is crowded with generic "flat stomach" content that rarely addresses glute development specifically, and Marrero's dual focus, shrinking the midsection while building the glutes rather than treating fat loss as one undifferentiated goal, gives Método CLEAN a more specific hook than most competing free communities in the same niche.
