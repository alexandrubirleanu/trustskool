# Solda Fades and MANTRA: The Hair Influencer With 1.3 Million TikTok Followers Building a Product Line

MANTRA | Men's Hair & Style is a free Skool community of roughly 3,770 members positioned as "The #1 Hub for the Polished Gentleman," covering hair, fashion, fragrance, fitness and skincare for men. The community's Skool About page credits Solda Fades as its founder.

## Who is Solda Fades

Solda Fades, who goes by Vince publicly, is a hair and style influencer with a substantial following built across multiple platforms: 1.3 million followers and 38.9 million likes on TikTok alone, plus active YouTube and Instagram channels ([TikTok](https://www.tiktok.com/@soldafades), [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/soldafades/), [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@soldafades)). His content mixes practical men's haircare advice, styling tutorials, and troubleshooting for common issues like dandruff and dry scalp, with occasional personal reflections on faith and values threaded through his posts.

That content style, hands-on styling demonstrations rather than purely aspirational imagery, has translated into a physical product: MANTRA now sells its own styling comb, the MANTRA Definition Comb, available across the UK, US, EU, Australia and UAE markets.

## What members actually get

Inside MANTRA, members get access to community discussion around hairstyling techniques, product recommendations, and hair transformations, alongside direct posts from Solda Fades himself covering things like seasonal styling approaches, new product launches, and troubleshooting for specific hair types and textures. The community functions as a natural extension of the same content he already posts to his social channels, but with more direct community interaction than a one-way social feed allows.

Product posts inside the community follow a similar rhythm to his public content, comb reviews, new-styling demonstrations, and early-access announcements for products like the Definition Comb, which gives paying customers of the physical product a reason to stick around in the free community rather than treating it as a one-time purchase relationship.

## Why it's grown

MANTRA's growth follows a familiar pattern for creator-led Skool communities: build a large, engaged social media audience first through consistent, practical content, then convert a slice of that audience into a more direct community relationship on a platform built for exactly that. With 1.3 million TikTok followers already primed on his styling advice and product opinions, moving a portion of that audience into a free Skool space, and eventually into his own physical product line, is a much shorter trust-building path than starting a community from zero. The product line itself, the Definition Comb rolling out across five international markets, also suggests the community and broader brand have reached a scale where physical retail expansion makes commercial sense.

For a men's grooming niche that's often dominated by either purely aspirational influencer content or purely commercial product marketing, a free community built around practical, testable advice, and run by someone whose day job is literally demonstrating the techniques on camera, gives MANTRA a more grounded reason for members to stick around than a typical brand-run Skool page.
