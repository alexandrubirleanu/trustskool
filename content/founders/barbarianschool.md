# Cohnan Kotarski and Barbarian School: Inside Skool's Fat-Loss Community for Career-Driven Men

Barbarian School is a $25-a-month Skool community with just over 3,200 members, pitched around losing 10 to 20 pounds without giving up favorite foods or living in the gym. The coach behind it, Cohnan Kotarski, built his brand under the name Barbarian Physique.

## Who is Cohnan Kotarski

Kotarski grew up in the Netherlands as, in his own words, an insecure and overweight child, then moved to Canada at age 11, where hockey and basketball gave him an early competitive outlet before he started training seriously at 16. He later earned a soccer scholarship to MacEwan University and went on to get certified as a personal trainer, turning what started as a personal transformation story into an online coaching business ([LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/cohnan-kotarski-7462a854/)). His personal website lays out that same coaching identity, positioning him specifically around helping career-driven men lose 30 to 50 pounds without sacrificing their favorite foods or spending hours in the gym ([cohnankotarski.com](https://cohnankotarski.com/)).

He's built a following across Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, and X under variations of his own name and the Barbarian Physique brand, and Myprotein featured him as a brand ambassador, publishing a piece under his name covering his top weight-loss recommendations ([Myprotein feature](https://us.myprotein.com/thezone/our-ambassadors/top-3-weight-loss-hacks-with-cohnan-kotarski/)). He also hosts The Barbarian Show, a podcast built around helping men reach their health and mindset goals, with an episode literally titled "From Chubby Kid to Fitness Coach" retelling his own origin story.

## What members actually get

Barbarian School's core offer is flexible eating plans built around fat loss and muscle building without an all-or-nothing approach to food, paired with workouts designed around a lean, functional physique rather than pure strength or bodybuilding numbers. Mindset content sits alongside the physical programming, reflecting Kotarski's own framing of his transformation as much psychological as physical.

At $25 a month, the community sits at a low-commitment price point relative to typical one-on-one online coaching, positioning it as an accessible middle tier between free content and Kotarski's presumably pricier individual coaching offers.

## Why it's grown

Kotarski's own documented transformation, from an insecure overweight kid to a brand ambassador with over a million followers across platforms, gives Barbarian School a founder story that's been externally validated by a real supplement brand rather than only self-reported. That kind of third-party endorsement is a meaningful trust signal in a fitness niche crowded with unverifiable before-and-after claims.

The specific target audience, career-driven men who don't have hours to spend in the gym, also narrows the pitch enough to stand out from generic weight loss communities, giving Barbarian School a clearer identity than a broad "get fit" positioning would.
