# Eileen and Harrison Wilder's ICONS Online: A Free Skool Hub for a Speaking and Sales Empire

ICONS Online is a free Skool community with around 2,230 members, pitched at "entrepreneurs and aspiring entrepreneurs who are called to be a source of inspiration for their world." It isn't a standalone product so much as a shared classroom: everyone inside has bought at least one paid resource from Eileen or Harrison Wilder, and the community exists to give those buyers a place to keep learning together.

## Who are Eileen and Harrison Wilder

Eileen Wilder spent more than two decades as a pastor before a difficult ministry transition in 2017 forced a change of direction, according to her own site ([eileenwilder.com/about-me](https://eileenwilder.com/about-me/)). She has described being "deathly afraid of public speaking" at the time, yet within roughly two years had turned speaking into what she calls a million-dollar business. Industry coverage has picked up the same arc: a December 2022 profile on Jeff Heggie's blog details her jump from pastoral income to charging over $55,000 for VIP consulting days and building a program that promises five- and six-figure results from a single virtual event ([jeffheggie.com](https://www.jeffheggie.com/blogs/blog/million-dollar-speaker-eileen-wilder)). She's since been billed as "The Queen of Stages" on a dedicated podcast episode ([Apple Podcasts](https://podcasts.apple.com/us/podcast/eileen-wilder-the-queen-of-stages/id1617464092?i=1000585278927)) and has shared stages with figures like Russell Brunson and Tony Robbins per that same coverage. Her companies include Confident Closer®, Speaking School™, Virtual Event Secrets™, and Elite Entrepreneurs™.

Harrison Wilder, her husband, has his own separate professional identity: he holds an Executive MBA from George Washington University and serves as Executive Pastor at Capital City Church, a multicultural congregation in Washington, D.C., where he's also known as an author and speaker on leadership, finances, and generational relationships ([CelebrityPress bio](https://celebritypresspublishing.com/members/harrison-wilder/)). A joint YouTube video, "Harrison and Eileen Wilder | M3 Changed Everything for Us," documents the couple's history at the M3 conference dating back to 2017 ([YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=JeFmnVtig9c)).

## What members actually get

Because ICONS Online is free to join, the actual teaching content sits behind whatever paid course or coaching product got each member in the door, whether that's a Speaking School program, a Confident Closer offer, or one of Harrison's leadership resources. Inside Skool, the community functions as the ongoing support layer: a shared classroom and peer group for people who've already invested in the Wilders' paid material and want continued access to each other and to updates from Eileen and Harrison directly.

## Why it's grown

This is a recognizable pattern for established course creators moving onto Skool: rather than selling community access as the primary product, the Wilders use it as retention infrastructure for an existing paid funnel. Eileen's public arc, pastor to million-dollar speaker in under two years, is the kind of specific, well-documented transformation story that travels well on podcasts and blogs, and that outside coverage does real work bringing in buyers before they ever see the Skool page. Harrison's parallel credibility as an Executive Pastor with an MBA adds a second, distinct authority angle rather than duplicating hers. Folding thousands of existing customers into one free Skool space then gives the couple a low-cost way to keep cross-selling across their various programs without running a separate community for each one.
