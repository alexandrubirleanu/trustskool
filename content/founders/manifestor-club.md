# Elisha Crowd and Manifestor Club: Inside Skool's Largest German-Language Manifestation Community

Manifestor Club is a free Skool community with more than 7,500 members, described by its own founder as the largest manifestation community in the German-speaking world. It's run by Elisha Crowd, a coach and speaker who has built a large following under the personal brand "Sonne im Kopf."

## Who is Elisha Crowd

Elisha has more than 133,000 followers on Instagram, where he posts under the Sonne im Kopf brand focused on psychology, spirituality, and manifestation ([Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/elishacrowd/)). He also runs a YouTube channel under his own name and has appeared as an interview guest discussing personal branding, including an episode titled "Wie Du mit Personal Branding erfolgreich wirst" ([YouTube interview](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KeX7Nh2rlDY)). According to his own bio, he has more than 20 years of experience as a coach, mentor, and speaker, and says he has worked directly with more than 10,000 people through live events.

His public content centers on what he calls energy work and conscious manifestation, framed as a system for understanding what he describes as universal laws rather than generic self-help affirmations, and his current material is built around a program he calls "Manifestation 3.0," pitched as a way to work with a specific year's energy rather than a one-size-fits-all method. Alongside his online content, he also runs in-person live events under the Sonne im Kopf brand, which is where he says the bulk of that 10,000-person figure comes from.

## What members actually get

Manifestor Club is free to join and gives members a structured entry point into Elisha's manifestation framework, including a step-by-step formula for applying manifestation intentionally, a downloadable guide explaining the underlying concepts in simple terms, and audio material like guided meditations and subliminal affirmation tracks.

## Why it's grown

Manifestation and law-of-attraction content has a large, pre-existing audience online, and Elisha's advantage is that he already had a following of well over 100,000 people before building the Skool community, giving him a ready-made pipeline of people to invite in for free. Two decades of coaching experience and a stated 10,000-plus people reached through live events also give him more standing in this space than most manifestation-focused creators, many of whom have little public track record beyond their own social posts. As with several large manifestation communities on Skool, the free tier appears designed to build a big, engaged base that can later be pointed toward paid coaching, live events, or higher-ticket programs.
