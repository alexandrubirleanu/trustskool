# Nurse Sam and Camp Nursing School: The LPN Behind Skool's NCLEX Prep Community

Camp Nursing School bills itself as the meeting point of nursing education and camping spirit, a free Skool community with more than 2,500 members ranging from first-year nursing students to people cramming for the NCLEX. The person running it goes by Nurse Sam, an LPN who built a whole study-guide brand around the idea that students "retain when they're entertained."

## Who is Nurse Sam

Sam Cepeda is a Licensed Practical Nurse and the founder and CEO of Nurse Sam Global Media LLC, the company behind both TheNurseSam.com and the Camp Nursing School community ([About Us page, thenursesam.com](https://thenursesam.com/pages/about-us)). Her own site describes launching the brand roughly a month after undergoing an emergency hysterectomy, calling the business her "literal baby and only child" in that same account of the company's origin.

Her stated approach to nursing education rejects dry, text-heavy study materials in favor of visually driven, nostalgia-inflected study guides and interactive case studies, built specifically for what she describes as visual learners preparing for exams like the ATI TEAS and NCLEX. She's also outspoken about respect for LPNs specifically, using the hashtag #LPNsAreNursesPeriod across her social channels to push back against what she sees as the profession being talked down to within nursing more broadly.

## What members actually get

Camp Nursing School functions as the free, community-facing front door to Cepeda's broader nursing education business. Members get access to a "global digital campfire," per the community's own framing, aimed at students anywhere along the pipeline from beginner nursing courses through NCLEX prep. That typically means peer support, study accountability, and pointers toward the paid study guides and NCLEX bundles sold through her main site and referenced in student-shared materials like the NCLEX bundle documents circulating on study platforms such as Studocu.

The "camp" branding is deliberate: rather than positioning nursing school prep as pure grind, the community leans into a lighter, more social tone, which fits Cepeda's stated philosophy that engagement drives retention better than dense textbook review.

## Why it's grown

Nurse Sam's growth pattern mirrors a common one among niche professional-education creators: she built an audience and a study-guide product line first, through her own site and social channels, before Skool became a free layer for community and lead generation on top of that existing brand. Her advocacy angle around LPN recognition also gives the community a point of view beyond generic exam tips, which helps it stand out among the many NCLEX-prep spaces competing for the same students.

Because nursing students are a highly time-pressured, exam-driven audience actively searching for study resources, a free community that doubles as a funnel into paid guides is a natural fit, and Cepeda's personal story of building the business through a serious medical event gives the brand a resilience narrative that resonates with students going through their own hard stretches.
