# Omar Msaddi and Scrum Master Hackers: Turning 12 Years of Agile Coaching Into a Career-Change Roadmap

Scrum Master Hackers, offered on Skool with a 7-day trial before converting to a $27-a-month membership, promises a proven 3-step system for becoming an AI-empowered Scrum Master in 7 days and landing interviews within 30. It's built and run by Omar Msaddi, credited directly as the founder on the community's About page.

## Who is Omar Msaddi

Omar Msaddi is an Agile Coach and SPC-certified trainer (Scaled Agile Framework Practice Consultant), with roughly 12 years of experience working as a Scrum Master and Agile Coach before turning to teaching others how to break into the field. He maintains a public professional presence as an Agile Coach on Facebook and LinkedIn, where he regularly posts practical content on Scrum Master fundamentals and career advice, and he's active on Instagram sharing short-form content on why the Scrum Master role is evolving rather than disappearing, as some industry commentary has suggested ([Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/AgileCoachOmar/); [LinkedIn post](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/omarmsaddi_%3F%3F%3F%3F-%3F%3F-%3F%3F%3F%3F-%3F%3F-%3F%3F%3F%3F-%3F-activity-7388649030681174016-LMCZ)). He publicly announced the launch of the Scrum Master Hackers Skool community as a free offering in a YouTube video, before it evolved into the current trial-then-paid structure ([launch video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SMBGTOW41ag)).

## What members actually get

The community centers on what Msaddi calls the SMH 3-Step Roadmap, a structured path built from his 12 years of hands-on Agile coaching experience, aimed specifically at helping career-changers land Scrum Master roles quickly. According to the community's own materials, the roadmap has already helped nearly 4,000 professionals transition into Scrum Master careers, positioning it as one of the more established career-transition communities in the agile coaching niche on Skool.

New members get a 7-day trial before the membership converts to its $27-a-month price, which gives prospective career-changers a short window to sample the roadmap and community discussion before committing financially, a lower-risk structure than a straight upfront paywall.

## Why it's grown

Msaddi's community sits at an interesting moment for the Scrum Master role: there's real public debate, some of which he directly engages with in his own content, over whether the role is being phased out as organizations flatten their agile practices or fold Scrum Master responsibilities into other positions. By positioning his roadmap around becoming an "AI-empowered" Scrum Master rather than a traditional one, Msaddi is directly addressing that anxiety, giving prospective members a reason to believe the career path still has room to grow rather than shrink. Combined with over a decade of direct coaching experience to back the roadmap's credibility, that positioning gives the community a clear, timely hook in a niche where a lot of competing career advice feels increasingly uncertain.

His own content, short-form videos on platforms like Instagram breaking down why the role is evolving rather than dying, also does double duty as both marketing and a preview of the roadmap's underlying argument, which likely helps convert skeptical scrollers into trial sign-ups.
