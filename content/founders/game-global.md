# Ice White, Kieren Brown, Joe Elvin and Game Global: Skool's Largest Wingman Network

Game Global describes itself plainly as the home of the seduction community, and its Skool space, with over 2,500 members, is built around what it calls the biggest network of wingmen in the world for dating and "infield" practice. The community traces back to three named coaches: Ice White, Kieren Brown, and Joe Elvin, each with years of public track record in dating coaching before Game Global moved onto Skool.

## Who runs Game Global

Ice White is described on the company's own site as the figure behind Game Global's network of local "Game Cities" and the author of the book *The Message Game*, alongside creating MGAI, an AI dating assistant built for online dating platforms ([Game Global coaches page](https://www.gameglobal.net/coaches/)). Joe Elvin works as a dating and relationships writer who travels internationally practicing and teaching day game across different cultures, per that same page.

Kieren Brown has been active in the space for close to two decades, and has been profiled by local press as helping men rebuild dating confidence, described in one feature as empowering men to reclaim confidence in modern dating ([Grapevine Birmingham profile](https://www.grapevinebirmingham.com/navigating-modern-romance-kieren-brown-empowers-men-to-reclaim-confidence-in-dating/)). Brown also writes for the company's own Medium publication under the Game Global byline ([Medium, Game Global](https://medium.com/game-global/elite-dating-advice-gives-italian-man-endless-female-connections-356dbc386bd8)) and has an upcoming book, *The Primal Gentleman*, focused on authenticity and emotional intelligence in dating.

## What members actually get

Game Global's site describes the community filling a gap left when Real Social Dynamics, an earlier major player in pickup-artist coaching, shut down around 2019. Since then, the network has grown to more than 310 local "Game Cities," some with over 1,000 members each, where men meet up to practice social approaches together rather than going out alone. On Skool specifically, the community runs like an active forum layered with free courses, weekly classes, and a points system that unlocks additional course content, along with an AI wingman tool.

A "WINGMAN MAP" feature lets members find others nearby to go out with, which is the structural core of the "wingman network" pitch, turning an otherwise solitary and often intimidating activity into something done in groups.

## Why it's grown

Game Global's growth rests on two things: multiple established coaches bringing their own existing audiences into one shared platform, rather than a single creator building from zero, and a genuine structural niche, local, in-person practice groups, that most online dating-advice content doesn't address at all. Both Ice White's published book and AI tool, and Kieren Brown's near two-decade track record with independent press coverage, give the brand more third-party-verifiable history than most communities in this space.

The gamified points system and city-based local groups also give members a reason to stay active well beyond a single course purchase, which is a structurally different retention model than most single-coach content communities.
