# Mark Wickersham and AI Community: The Chartered Accountant Teaching Firms to Use AI

AI Community, with well over 3,300 members, positions itself as a free resource specifically for accountants and bookkeepers who want to use ChatGPT and other AI tools to streamline their firms and boost profitability. It's run by Mark Wickersham, a name well known in accounting circles for a different specialty entirely: pricing.

## Who is Mark Wickersham

Wickersham is a UK chartered accountant, author, and public speaker who's spent roughly 25 years teaching accountants how to stop trading hours for dollars through value pricing rather than hourly billing. He founded the Value Pricing Academy, where he currently mentors more than 400 accountants, and hosts The Value Pricing Podcast, distributed on Apple Podcasts under his own name ([Value Pricing Academy](https://www.valuepricingacademy.com/); [The Value Pricing Podcast](https://podcasts.apple.com/us/podcast/the-value-pricing-podcast/id1616172900)). Software company Ignition features him on its own team and partner page, describing him as the most sought-after profit improvement expert in the accounting profession ([Ignition](https://www.ignitionapp.com/team/mark-wickersham)).

More recently, Wickersham has pivoted a meaningful part of his teaching toward AI specifically, and by his own account has shown several hundred accountants how to use AI tools to do more with less over the last few years, a natural extension of his existing profit-and-efficiency focus rather than an unrelated new venture.

## What members actually get

Inside AI Community, members get access to structured AI courses built for an accounting context, monthly live workshops and tutorials, a peer community of other accountants and bookkeepers working through the same material, and access to a prompt library tool designed to speed up common AI use cases in practice. Being free positions it as a top-of-funnel resource likely feeding into Wickersham's other paid programs, similar to how the Value Pricing Academy operates.

## Why it's grown

Wickersham arrives at AI education for accountants with a quarter-century of existing credibility inside that exact profession, which is a meaningfully different starting position than a generic AI educator trying to break into a specialized niche cold. Accountants and bookkeepers are also a profession under real pressure to adopt AI tools quickly without compromising client confidentiality or accuracy, and having a recognized profession insider teach that adoption, rather than an outside AI generalist, likely reduces the skepticism firms bring to the topic.

Free access removes any financial barrier for firms still deciding whether AI adoption is worth their time, while Wickersham's existing podcast and Value Pricing Academy audience gives AI Community a built-in pipeline of accountants who already trust his teaching on a different topic.
