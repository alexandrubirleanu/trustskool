# Hayley Jennings and The Strategy: Inside Skool's 7,400-Member Instagram Growth Community

The Strategy is a free Skool community built around a single course promise: take a small Instagram account and turn it into rapid growth and daily sales. With roughly 7,466 members, it's one of the larger creator-marketing groups on the platform, and it sits behind a public identity that's easy to trace back to a real person: Hayley, known online as @selfwealthmom.

## Who is Hayley Jennings

Hayley Jennings runs her public presence under the handle @selfwealthmom, where her Instagram bio describes her as focused on social media strategy ([Instagram profile](https://www.instagram.com/selfwealthmom/)). The Skool community's own about page names her directly as the creator of The Strategy and lists a contact email, selfwealthmom@gmail.com, for members who run into access issues ([The Strategy on Skool](https://www.skool.com/the-strategy-7458/about)). She also runs a Beacons-style link-in-bio page under her handle that ties her various offers together ([selfwealthmom.live](https://selfwealthmom.live)).

Unlike a lot of anonymous "growth guru" accounts on Skool, Hayley's setup is straightforward: a real, findable Instagram account with a consistent name and handle across platforms, a public course sold through Stan Store, and a Skool community that functions as the free front door to that paid offer.

## What members actually get

The Skool group itself is free to join. Inside, it points members toward "The Strategy," a paid course purchased through a linked Stan Store page, described on the community's own listing as "the Instagram strategy that took the digital marketing world by storm" ([Stan Store listing](https://stan.store/selfwealthmom/p/the-strategy-8wma4ql2)). The pitch is narrow and specific: take an account that isn't getting traction and apply a repeatable strategy to grow it quickly and start generating consistent sales, rather than a general "how to use Instagram" course.

That structure, free community as the top of funnel, paid product as the actual offer, is a common pattern on Skool, but it only works if the person behind it has a real audience willing to follow them from free content into a paid purchase. Hayley's Instagram following and her own bio positioning as a digital marketer suggest that's exactly the audience this was built for.

## Why it's grown

At 7,466 members with no listed price to join, The Strategy behaves less like a standalone paid community and more like a lead-generation engine for Hayley's Instagram growth course. That's a deliberate tradeoff: a free Skool group is a much lower-friction ask than a paid one, so it can absorb a much larger volume of curious Instagram users, and Hayley only needs a fraction of them to convert to the paid Stan Store product to make the funnel worthwhile.

It also plays directly to her own credibility. Someone selling an "Instagram strategy" needs a visibly active, well-positioned Instagram account of their own, and @selfwealthmom's public profile does that work before a single member ever opens the Skool app.
