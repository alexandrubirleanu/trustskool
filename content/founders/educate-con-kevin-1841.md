# Kevin Rodriguez and Edúcate Con Kevin: Puerto Rico's Free Credit and Finance Classroom on Skool

Edúcate Con Kevin is a free, Spanish-language Skool community with around 2,350 members, welcoming people who want to learn about credit and personal finances. Its own about section frames the mission plainly: change how people in Puerto Rico talk about credit, investments, and money, and build the largest such community on the island.

## Who is Kevin Rodriguez

The full name behind the brand is Kevin Gabriel Rodríguez Hernández, a Puerto Rico-based creator who's built a substantial following, around 190,000 on Instagram under @kevinrodhernandez, around content covering credit-card optimization, travel hacking and retirement planning ([Instagram profile](https://www.instagram.com/kevinrodhernandez/)). He runs a YouTube channel under his own name and has a video specifically framed around his Puerto Rico finance work, titled "Joven Inversionista lidera las Finanzas Personales en Puerto Rico" ([YouTube video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DfvLLU3PtlY)). He's also made the rounds as a guest on Spanish-language finance podcasts, including an episode of "Master your money habits" covering his approach to personal finance in Puerto Rico ([Spotify episode](https://creators.spotify.com/pod/show/infusion-investments/episodes/107-Joven-Inversionista-hace-lo-que-NADIE-QUIERE-en-Puerto-Rico-Educate-con-Kevin-e1k8sb0)) and an appearance on "Cereal Empresarial con Miguel Contés" specifically about saving money on flights and hotels using credit-card points ([Spotify episode](https://creators.spotify.com/pod/show/miguelcontes/episodes/115-Kevin-Rodrguez--Cmo-Ahorrarte-MILES-de--en-Vuelos-y-Hoteles-con-Tarjetas-de-Crdito-e2hiafe)).

## What members actually get

Per the community's own description, members unlock weekly live calls and additional courses for free by engaging, commenting, asking questions, and participating with other members' posts, rather than paying for tiered access. That structure rewards active participation over passive lurking, which fits a group built around a specifically local, engagement-driven mission rather than a one-time course sale.

## Why it's grown

Kevin's Instagram following, built specifically around a niche that gets far less dedicated coverage than mainstream US personal-finance content, gives Edúcate Con Kevin a built-in audience with an unusually clear local identity. Podcast guest appearances extend that reach further, putting him in front of listeners who follow other Puerto Rican finance and entrepreneurship shows and then funnel back into his free Skool group. His content on credit-card points and travel hacking in particular has circulated widely enough on Instagram and TikTok to be described as viral in coverage of his work, which likely keeps a steady stream of new followers discovering him outside of any single platform. The engagement-gated content model (comment and participate to unlock live calls) is also a lightweight way to keep a free community active rather than letting it go quiet after the initial sign-up spike, something plenty of larger, better-funded Skool groups still struggle to pull off.
