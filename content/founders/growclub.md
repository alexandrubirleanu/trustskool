# Cristopher Clavero and growclub: Inside Skool's 2,700-Member B2B Growth Community

growclub describes itself, in Spanish, as the knowledge hub for selling more through funnels, ads, and AI. It's free to join and has built a community of roughly 2,752 members, positioning itself as the top B2B customer acquisition academy for Latin America.

## Who runs it

The founder is Cristopher Clavero, based in Santiago, Chile, whose LinkedIn profile lists him as founder of growclub with roughly twelve years spent helping companies acquire customers in a predictable, scalable way ([LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/cristopher-clavero/)). Business data aggregator RocketReach independently lists Clavero as growclub's founder and management contact, separate from growclub's own marketing ([RocketReach](https://rocketreach.co/cristopher-clavero-email_102556922)). Clavero also runs Jungla Brands, described on his LinkedIn as a holding company for building and scaling digital B2B and D2C companies across Latin America, and posts regularly to growclub's own YouTube channel and to LinkedIn on cold email and prospecting tactics ([YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UC9HVisoWskqUgK9SSC5q3Wg/about); [LinkedIn post](https://es.linkedin.com/posts/cristopher-clavero_todos-quieren-m%C3%A1s-reuniones-de-ventas-activity-7341462513395339267-599j)). RocketReach's org chart for growclub also lists Lucas Clavero in a growth role and Thomas Caignard as business development manager, suggesting the operation extends beyond a single founder posting under a personal brand ([RocketReach](https://rocketreach.co/growclub-management_b6f7618fc6b385a7)).

## What members actually get

growclub's stated focus is the vocabulary and tooling of B2B pipeline generation: funnels, leads, meetings, landing pages, cold email, paid ads, and automation, aimed at founders and marketers whose businesses depend on booking meetings rather than driving straight e-commerce sales. The positioning is explicitly practical, framed as moving from strategy to the actual operation of campaigns rather than staying at the level of theory, which fits Clavero's own LinkedIn posting habits: his public posts walk through specific cold-email sending volumes and prospecting mechanics rather than staying at the level of motivational advice.

## Why it's grown

B2B lead generation is a durable pain point across Latin American markets that haven't been saturated with English-language growth content the way the US market has, and growclub's Spanish-language, funnel-and-ads-specific angle gives it a clearer wedge than a generic "grow your business" community would have. Clavero's twelve-year operating history, corroborated outside his own community by both LinkedIn and RocketReach rather than resting solely on growclub's marketing copy, gives the community a credibility anchor that's harder to fake than a Skool bio alone. Free access keeps the entry barrier low for entrepreneurs testing whether cold outreach and paid funnels are worth investing in before they consider Clavero's likely paid offerings around done-for-you campaign build-out.
