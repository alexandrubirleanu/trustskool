# Pol Morado and Academia Growing Inmobiliario: The Agent Behind Skool's Free Real Estate Community

Academia Growing Inmobiliario markets itself as the number one academy for real estate agents, currently free, with over 3,200 members and a specific promise: get an exclusive listing at the right price and sell it within 30 days. It's run by Pol Morado.

## Who is Pol Morado

Morado runs a YouTube channel under the name "Pol Morado Inmobiliario," publishing content aimed at real estate agents in the Spanish-speaking market ([Pol Morado Inmobiliario on YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCX-ohNXGDjIhEuy9rOdypig)). His Instagram account, under his own name, identifies him as the founder of aducion.co, a business focused on helping real estate operations scale, giving him a documented professional identity beyond the Skool community itself ([Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/polmorado/)).

Inside the community, Morado is credited with having helped more than 2,000 agents and having signed over 1,000 exclusive listings using his own acquisition script, a specific, checkable claim rather than a vague success metric, and he co-runs weekly Q&A sessions alongside a partner named Juanca.

## What members actually get

The academy centers on Morado's acquisition script, the specific pitch and process he says agents can use to win exclusive representation on a listing at the right asking price, paired with weekly live Q&A sessions where he and Juanca work through agents' real situations. The 30-day sale promise tied to the acquisition script gives the community a concrete, time-bound outcome rather than an open-ended promise to "grow your real estate business."

## Why it's grown

Winning exclusive listings, rather than competing against other agents on the same open property, is one of the most consistently sought-after skills in residential real estate, and a script-based system with a specific, quantified track record, 2,000-plus agents helped, gives Morado's pitch more concrete backing than most generic real estate coaching offers. Free access also removes the upfront cost barrier for agents who are curious but not ready to invest in paid coaching.

Having an existing YouTube channel and Instagram presence under his own name and business, aducion.co, also gives Academia Growing Inmobiliario a discovery channel that doesn't depend entirely on organic growth inside Skool.

The real estate niche also tends to reward agents who can show a repeatable system rather than one-off success stories, since most agents are competing on the same local inventory and the same basic listing tactics. A named, checkable acquisition script gives Morado's audience something concrete to evaluate before committing time to the academy, which matters more in a commission-driven profession than in hobbyist niches where the cost of trying something new is mostly just time.
