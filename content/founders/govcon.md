# Saint Peguero and GovconOS: Inside Skool's 14,000-Member Government Contracting Community

GovconOS is a Skool community with more than 14,000 members built around a single, high-stakes goal: helping small businesses win their first, or their next, US government contract. Unusually for this list, it charges just $1 for lifetime access rather than a recurring subscription. It's run by Saint Peguero, who built his credibility inside government contracting itself before turning to education.

## Who is Saint Peguero

Saint Peguero's positioning centers on more than 11 years of direct experience as a General Services Administration (GSA) prime contractor, during which he says he executed contracts totaling more than $15.6 million ([GovconOS's own about page](https://www.skool.com/govcon/about)). He describes his transition from operator to educator as moving from being a contractor "under a GSA prime" to becoming an acquisition strategist, building proprietary systems and studying federal procurement regulation in depth in order to teach others what he calls proposal strategies that contracting officers actually approve and award.

That's a meaningfully different founder profile than most communities on this list: rather than building an audience through content first and monetizing it later, Peguero appears to have built his credibility through direct, dollar-verifiable work inside the government contracting system, then packaged that operational experience into an education product. Independent, third-party reporting on Peguero specifically is limited outside his own platform's claims, which is worth noting plainly, but the specificity of his stated track record ($15.6M+ across 11+ years under a named contract vehicle type) is more concrete than the vaguer "I built a 7-figure business" claims common elsewhere in the online education space.

## What members actually get

GovconOS is aimed squarely at service providers, consultants, suppliers, and agencies trying to break into government contracting as a client channel. Members get daily local, state, and federal contract opportunity listings, tools and frameworks for structuring competitive proposals, direct strategy sessions, and AI-powered automation aimed at speeding up the notoriously paperwork-heavy proposal process. The government contracting niche is distinctive in that the "customer" (government agencies) operates under public, rules-based procurement processes, which means a lot of the value in a community like this is less about sales technique and more about correctly navigating bureaucratic requirements that most first-time bidders get wrong.

## Why it's grown

Government contracting is an enormous, durable market. Government contracting represents one of the largest addressable buyer markets in the US economy, and yet it remains poorly understood by most small business owners, who are often intimidated by the paperwork and procurement rules rather than the underlying sales opportunity. A $1 lifetime-access price point removes essentially all financial friction to trying the community, which is a smart match for an audience of cautious small-business owners and consultants who may be skeptical of "gurus" in this specific niche given how many contracting scams and low-quality courses have circulated online.

Peguero's specific, numbers-based credibility (a stated contract value, a stated years-of-experience figure, a named contract vehicle) gives GovconOS a more verifiable-feeling pitch than much of the online business education space, even though full independent verification of those figures isn't publicly available.
