# Andrew Naegele and AI Simple: Inside Skool's 5,800-Member AI Marketing Community

AI Simple is a free Skool community of about 5,781 members for founders who want to use AI to ship offers, content, and campaigns faster, without getting lost in complex prompt engineering. It's run by Andrew Naegele, who built his reputation helping businesses automate lead generation before turning that expertise into a community.

## Who is Andrew Naegele

Andrew Naegele's LinkedIn profile lists his current focus as AI Simple, matching the Skool community directly ([LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/andrewnaegele/)). He's also documented publicly as COO at Lead Gen Jay, a business focused on AI and automation for B2B lead generation and cold outreach, and posts regularly on LinkedIn about using AI to improve cold email performance and automate outreach workflows. His public LinkedIn activity going back several years covers topics like AI-driven cold email systems and automated lead generation, consistent with the practical, workflow-focused framing AI Simple uses.

The Skool community's own about page describes him building AI systems for founders in the seven-to-nine-figure range, and running a recurring live call series called "THE TABLE" where he walks through testing results and workflows directly with members.

## What members actually get

AI Simple is free to join and is built for solopreneurs and lean founders, particularly those already earning somewhere between $5,000 and $50,000 a month, who want practical AI workflows rather than theoretical AI education. The pitch centers on plug-and-play automations and templates that let founders ship content, offers, and campaigns in hours rather than weeks, with Naegele's live "THE TABLE" calls functioning as the main mechanism for sharing new workflows and answering questions as tools and tactics change.

That focus on speed and simplicity over technical depth positions the community differently than more developer-oriented AI communities, aiming instead at business owners who want AI to remove busywork rather than become AI specialists themselves.

## Why it's grown

At 5,781 members and free to join, AI Simple benefits from riding a genuinely fast-moving wave: founders across nearly every industry are actively looking for ways to use AI tools without needing to learn to code, and Naegele's background running AI-driven lead generation for other businesses gives him a credible, outcomes-focused angle rather than a purely theoretical one. His consistent LinkedIn posting history on cold email and automation topics, tied to a real operating role at Lead Gen Jay, gives prospective members a way to evaluate his practical experience before joining, rather than taking community claims purely at face value.
