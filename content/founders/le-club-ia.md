# Thomas Berton and Le Club IA: Inside Skool's 15,000-Member French AI Community

Le Club IA has grown to roughly 15,000 members on Skool by positioning itself as the largest French-speaking community dedicated to AI and automation, offering free n8n templates and practical guides alongside a paid VIP tier. Its founder, Thomas Berton, built the audience that fills it through a parallel career as an AI consultant and agency owner.

## Who is Thomas Berton

Thomas Berton describes himself as an AI consultant whose mission is to make artificial intelligence accessible to people who aren't developers, a philosophy he lays out on his personal site ([thomas-berton.com](https://thomas-berton.com/a-propos)). He is co-founder, alongside Philémon Gavoty, of Azuro AI, an agency the company's own team page says has delivered more than 90 AI projects over three years, with Berton acting as CEO and self-described "AIOS architect" ([azuro-ai.com/equipe](https://azuro-ai.com/equipe)). He also founded Awilix, an AI marketing agency, and runs VibeSocials, a social-media automation product still in beta. Independent write-ups place his YouTube following above 40,000 subscribers, built through weekly videos and blog content on AI tools and automation, and a Skoolmakers review of Le Club IA credits him with roughly 20,000 YouTube subscribers at the time of that review and confirms he founded the community ([Skoolmakers review](https://skoolmakers.fr/communautes/le-club-ia/)). His LinkedIn profile lists the same set of ventures under his name ([LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/thomas-berton-563894196/)).

## What members actually get

The free tier of Le Club IA gives members access to n8n automation templates pulled directly from Berton's YouTube channel, along with practical guides covering business automation, AI phone agents, chatbot building and AI-assisted content generation. The paid VIP tier, priced around $79 a month, adds more than 70 video trainings, weekly live masterclasses with replays, monthly hackathons with prizes, and priority support, plus professional-grade templates for n8n, Make, Vapi and ManyChat. Le Club IA is also listed as an official n8n partner, which gives the community a degree of platform-level credibility beyond Berton's own following.

## Why it's grown

Le Club IA's growth mirrors the broader pattern among successful French-language AI communities on Skool: a founder who already runs a real AI agency, with real client work and a real YouTube audience, uses that credibility to build a community rather than starting from a course-only premise. Berton's positioning, that most people need five tools they actually use rather than forty they don't, gives the community a simpler, less overwhelming entry point than competitors chasing every new AI tool release.

The n8n partnership adds another layer of trust that's harder for smaller, unaffiliated AI communities in the same space to replicate, and the free-to-VIP structure lets Le Club IA convert a portion of its free base into recurring VIP revenue without gating basic access behind a paywall, which likely helped it scale past the 7,000 free members Skoolmakers documented toward its current size.
