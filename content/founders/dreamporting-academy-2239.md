# Daniel Raphael and Dreamporting Tribe: Inside Skool's 5,000-Member Reality-Shifting Community

Dreamporting Tribe is a Skool community of just over 5,000 members that promises to help people "break free of challenges," step into "full expression," and "quantum leap" their life and destiny. It's the free front door to a coaching business built by Daniel Raphael, who's been working in this space for close to two decades.

## Who is Daniel Raphael

By his own account, Raphael was born with unusually strong intuitive abilities that were later disrupted by trauma, prompting a 13-year global search across healing traditions and what his site calls ancient mystery school lineages before he began teaching what he'd learned ([dreamporting.com/dreamporting-about](https://dreamporting.com/dreamporting-about)). He describes over 18 years working at the intersection of consciousness, emotional regulation, and applied transformation, and says he's guided more than 50,000 students worldwide, including entrepreneurs, creatives, and senior leaders inside Fortune 500 organizations ([dreamporting.com](https://www.dreamporting.com/)).

He's been covered in press as the "Billionaire's Wizard" for his work supporting tech founders through emotional development, and his site lists an appearance presenting to world leaders at Necker Island. His client list includes Netflix producer Adi Shankar, credited on the site with calling the work an "operating system upgrade," and Justin Kathan, co-founder of the company Joist ([dreamporting.com](https://www.dreamporting.com/)). He's discussed his personal history and the Dreamporting method on multiple podcasts, including a detailed episode of The Great Unlearn titled "Materialize Your Dreams Through Dreamporting" ([The Great Unlearn, episode 93](https://podcasts.apple.com/us/podcast/93-daniel-raphael-materialize-your-dreams-through-dreamporting/id1492460338?i=1000575688932)) and an appearance on Let's Get Metaphysical ([Let's Get Metaphysical](https://www.letsgetmeta.com/dreamporting-how-to-dj-your-existence/)).

## What members actually get

The free Skool tribe functions as the top of a funnel leading into paid retreats with names like Inner Architect, Quest, Illumination, and Ascension, an online course called Frequency Temple, and one-on-one coaching, all built around Raphael's core method of using state and frequency-based techniques to clear what he describes as the internal blocks standing between someone and their goals.

## Why it's grown

Raphael's positioning leans on two things that are unusual for a Skool wellness community: a public client roster with names that are independently checkable, a Netflix producer, a startup co-founder, and nearly two decades of prior work and press coverage that existed before Dreamporting Tribe was ever a Skool page. For members drawn to manifestation and consciousness-work content, a founder who can point to Fortune 500-adjacent testimonials, rather than only anonymous member success stories, is a meaningfully different trust signal than most communities in this niche can offer. The free tribe lets that reputation reach a much wider audience than the paid retreats alone ever could before someone commits to a higher-priced program.
