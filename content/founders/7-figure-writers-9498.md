# Nicolas Cole and Dickie Bush: Inside Full Stack Writer, the Skool Community Behind Ship 30 for 30

Full Stack Writer is a Skool community of nearly 2,850 members built around a specific promise: acquire, combine, and monetize what its creators call the seven highest-leverage skills in digital writing today, delivered as eight self-paced mini-courses plus access to the 7-Figure Writers Skool group. The community's own Skool page credits it to "Nicolas Cole & Dickie Bush Support Team," the two writers behind the much larger writing education business Ship 30 for 30.

## Who are Nicolas Cole and Dickie Bush

Nicolas Cole built his public writing career the hard way, starting as a teenage gaming blogger and eventually becoming Quora's most-read writer, amassing millions of views before authoring "The Art and Business of Online Writing" and building a ghostwriting agency ([Nicolas Cole's story](https://www.nicolascole.com/post/the-story-of-nicolas-cole-my-overnight-success); [X @Nicolascole77](https://x.com/Nicolascole77)). Dickie Bush took a different path into the same world, a former hedge fund trader at BlackRock who left finance to teach digital writing full time ([The Business of Business profile](https://www.businessofbusiness.com/videos/meet-dickie-bush-the-25-year-old-finance-pro-launching-a-fleet-of-online-writers-ship-30-for-30/)).

The two partnered in January 2021 to launch Ship 30 for 30, packaging Cole's writing framework into a 30-day cohort course built around writing short, focused "atomic essays." By Cole's own account on X, the pair committed to working on nothing else for two years straight, and Ship 30 for 30 went on to train more than 10,000 students and generate what Cole describes as millions of dollars in revenue ([Nicolas Cole on X](https://x.com/Nicolascole77/status/1901589418223317376)).

## What members actually get

Full Stack Writer sits downstream of that main business, positioned as a self-paced expansion pack rather than the flagship cohort course itself. Members work through eight mini-courses covering the full range of monetizable digital writing skills, from ghostwriting to content strategy to building an audience, each delivered as combined text and video lessons, then get folded into the 7-Figure Writers Skool community to connect with other writers working through the same material.

That structure lets Cole and Bush monetize their core teaching twice, once through the flagship Ship 30 for 30 cohort experience, and again through a self-paced product for writers who want the frameworks without the live cohort format.

## Why it's grown

Cole and Bush had already built one of the most recognized brands in the online writing education space before this specific Skool community existed, Ship 30 for 30's reputation as the fastest-growing cohort-based writing course on the internet gives Full Stack Writer instant credibility with anyone who's encountered their content on X, Substack, or through word of mouth in writing circles. Both founders also maintain large, active personal audiences built on the exact skill they're teaching, writing publicly and consistently, which functions as a constant, self-reinforcing advertisement for the paid products behind it.

Because the underlying skill, digital writing and ghostwriting as a monetizable trade, has only grown more relevant as more creators and professionals build public platforms, the addressable audience for Cole and Bush's teaching has kept expanding rather than saturating.
