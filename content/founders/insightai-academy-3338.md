# Jax Delorme, Nick Vasiliu and InsightAI Academy: Inside Skool's 12,000-Member AI Agency Community

InsightAI Academy has grown to more than 12,000 members on Skool as a free course and community teaching people to sell high-ticket AI infrastructure to local businesses. It's run by two Ontario-based digital marketers, Jax Delorme and Nick Vasiliu, who built the offer out of their own agency backgrounds before turning it into a teachable system.

## Who are Jax Delorme and Nick Vasiliu

Jaxen "Jax" Delorme is listed on LinkedIn as co-founder of InsightAI Solutions Ltd, based in the Kanata area of Ontario, Canada ([LinkedIn](https://ca.linkedin.com/in/jaxen-delorme-619559264)). He maintains a public Instagram account under @jax.delorme with the bio "AI Infrastructure Expert," and an Ippei.com review of the academy describes him as a digital marketer and agency owner who got into AI sales after running paid Meta and Google Ads for local businesses, including medical spas, through an earlier venture called New Exposure Media ([Ippei review](https://ippei.com/insightai-academy/)). Nick Vasiliu, based in the Burlington, Ontario area, runs a parallel Instagram account as @nick.vasiliu with the same "AI Infrastructure Expert" framing and posts under his own name on YouTube. The Ippei review credits both men with more than $600,000 in combined sales of AI systems to businesses before packaging that experience into InsightAI Academy.

## What members actually get

The free academy teaches the fundamentals of selling AI-powered systems, such as appointment setters, lead qualifiers and customer-support agents, to service businesses like roofers, real estate agencies and insurance brokers, with InsightAI handling the technical fulfillment side so members can focus on sales. A paid tier, InsightAI Academy Gold, costs around $2,000 a year and adds deeper training and support for members trying to scale toward the $80,000-a-month figures the founders cite as achievable. Trustpilot reviews of the broader InsightAI brand describe Delorme and Vasiliu as hands-on and supportive, offering direct advice to members building out their own agencies ([Trustpilot](https://www.trustpilot.com/review/insightai.me)).

## Why it's grown

InsightAI Academy's growth follows a now-familiar Skool pattern in the AI-agency space: two operators with a real, prior paid-ads agency background pivot into selling a specific, newer service, AI infrastructure for local businesses, and use their own documented results as the proof that anchors a free community. That's a meaningfully different starting point than communities built by people with no agency operating history at all, and it shows up in how the founders talk about fulfillment: rather than teaching members to build the AI systems themselves, InsightAI keeps that layer in-house and focuses the free education on sales, a split that lowers the technical barrier to entry for members without coding backgrounds.

Two co-founders operating in tandem, each maintaining a separate but identically branded public presence, also doubles the number of entry points, Instagram, YouTube, LinkedIn, into the same community, which likely helped the free tier scale past 12,000 members faster than a single-founder version of the same offer might have.
