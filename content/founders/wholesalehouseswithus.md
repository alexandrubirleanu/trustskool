# Joe Reilly and Wholesale Houses With Us: An 8-Year Wholesaler's AI-Powered Deal Community

Wholesale Houses With Us is a Skool community of nearly 4,000 members offering an A-Z wholesaling blueprint, an AI-powered repair cost estimator, scripts, and what it calls "insider content" alongside a collaborative deal-making community. Its founder, credited directly on the Skool About page, is Joe Reilly.

## Who is Joe Reilly

Joe Reilly is a real estate investor with roughly eight years of experience specifically in wholesaling, real estate flipping, and landlording, and he's closed over 600 deals across that career ([BiggerPockets forum discussion](https://www.biggerpockets.com/forums/93/topics/1141892-enterprises-elite-joe-reilly)). He's the CEO of Enterprises Elite, a platform structured around collaboration between investors: when a wholesaler submits a deal and it needs a buyer, Enterprises Elite connects them with one and the two parties split the outcome 50/50 as a joint venture rather than one side simply flipping the deal for a flat assignment fee.

He maintains an active public teaching presence documenting his deals and giving tactical advice directly, including a widely viewed YouTube feature on how he built his wholesaling income past $1,000,000 a year, along with an active TikTok and Instagram following where he posts real-time commentary on wholesaling opportunities ([YouTube feature](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8wXuboUftAU); [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/realjoereilly/); [TikTok](https://www.tiktok.com/@joereillyofficial)).

## What members actually get

The community's central tool is DASI, an AI-powered repair cost estimator that helps wholesalers evaluate a property's repair costs before submitting an offer, a step that's traditionally required either contractor experience or a lot of guesswork to get right. Alongside that tool, members get access to scripts for talking to sellers, insider content on running a wholesaling operation, and the collaborative deal-making structure that Enterprises Elite is built around, meaning members aren't just learning theory, they're plugged into an actual network built for splitting real deals.

For newer wholesalers especially, the repair estimator lowers one of the biggest barriers to making a confident offer on a distressed property: knowing roughly what it will cost to fix up before a buyer ever walks the property in person.

## Why it's grown

Reilly's community benefits from combining a genuine, seven-figure personal track record with a practical tool that solves a specific, recurring pain point in wholesaling: accurately estimating repair costs without a contractor on speed dial. Rather than positioning the community purely as education, the joint-venture, 50/50 buyer-matching structure gives members a direct path to actually closing deals through Enterprises Elite's network rather than only learning concepts and having to hunt down buyers entirely on their own. That combination, real track record, a useful AI tool, and a built-in deal network, gives the community a more tangible value proposition than a typical "learn to wholesale" course.

Reilly's ongoing social content, TikTok clips reacting to real deal scenarios, Instagram posts breaking down offers, also keeps the community visible to an audience well beyond Skool's own discovery feed, feeding a steady stream of newer wholesalers into the free tier.
