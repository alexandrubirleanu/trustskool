# Oscar Patel and Oscar's Community: Inside Skool's 7,400-Member Health-and-Looks Community

Oscar's Community is a 7,441-member Skool community built around a single core idea: "Health = Looks." Rather than teaching skincare or cosmetic fixes, it teaches fascia training, myofunctional exercises, breathing mechanics, and posture correction as the real levers behind how attractive someone appears [Skool](https://www.skool.com/oscars-community/about).

## Who runs it

The community is run by Oscar Patel, a UK-based content creator who has built an Instagram following of over 1.2 million on his account, primarily through videos on fascia correction, myofunctional therapy, and facial-muscle exercises [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/_oscarpatel_/). Patel began posting to Instagram in 2024 and grew quickly on the strength of dramatic before-and-after content, including a reel with over 45 million views showing his own physical transformation, and a video of his 58-year-old mother's transformation that passed 1 million views, per public creator-profile coverage [Famous Birthdays](https://www.famousbirthdays.com/people/oscar-patel.html). He has said he used natural, holistic techniques to address his own asthma, sleep apnea, facial asymmetry, and posture issues, and interned at myofunctional institutes as part of developing his method. He has also collaborated with Dr. Mike Mew, the orthodontist known for popularizing "mewing." Beyond his own social content and the Skool page, independently reported biographical detail (education, credentials, clinical training specifics) is limited, which is common for creators who built an audience primarily through short-form video rather than traditional media coverage.

## What members actually get

Oscar's Community teaches the connection between structural health — how your fascia, jaw, and breathing function — and how you look and age. The core philosophy is that most people trying to "look better" focus on surface-level fixes when the underlying issue is mechanical: poor breathing efficiency, tight fascia, and habitual posture patterns that shape the face and body over years. Members get guided exercise routines and habit changes rather than product recommendations, positioning the community as an alternative to cosmetic intervention for people willing to put in months of consistent practice.

## Why it's grown

"Looksmaxxing" and fascia/mewing content has been one of the fastest-growing wellness niches in short-form video over the past two years, and Patel's own visible transformation gives him a personal proof point that's rare in this space — most creators in the niche are demonstrating techniques on other people, not their own multi-year before-and-after. His collaboration with an established, credentialed figure like Dr. Mike Mew also lends the content more legitimacy than the average looksmaxxing account, which tends to circulate uncredited or pseudoscientific claims.
