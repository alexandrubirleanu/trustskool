## What members actually get

The approach is framed around buying and reselling products on Amazon rather than launching a private-label brand from scratch, positioning it as a faster, lower-investment entry point into Amazon selling than building an entirely new product line.

Azzouz's own YouTube content about building a nutrition brand on Amazon without bank financing reinforces the same core pitch the community makes: growing a real Amazon business without heavy upfront capital or advertising spend.
