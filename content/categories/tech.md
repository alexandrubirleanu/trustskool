### How many members does a good Skool tech community have?
There's no fixed number. A focused 300-person community of people actually shipping projects can outperform a 10,000-member group that's mostly lurkers. If you want a curriculum plus a community, Skool communities are often a better fit. If you just want fast, informal peer chat, dev-focused Discords may serve that better.

### Do Skool tech communities keep up with fast-moving fields like AI?
