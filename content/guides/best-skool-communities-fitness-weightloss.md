# Best Skool Communities for Fitness and Weight Loss in 2026

Fitness and weight loss is one of the largest categories on Skool by raw count, with 424 communities tracked in our dataset, but the niche is far from a single audience. Inside it sit general fat-loss and muscle-building groups, GLP-1 and perimenopause-specific hubs, bodybuilding coaching crews, and masculinity-and-fitness communities that treat physical training as one piece of a broader lifestyle. Almost all of them are free to join, which makes this one of the least paywalled niches on the platform, with the handful of paid exceptions usually serving a specific professional audience rather than general members. ## How to Pick Between Them

The single most useful filter in this niche is whether a community targets you specifically or a general audience. OSX and Total Weight Loss are broad by design and work well if you're early in a fitness or weight-loss goal, while GLP1 Enhanced HUB and VHM Weight Loss-Perimenopause exist because a general fitness group won't address medication interactions or hormonal shifts with any real specificity.

Don't assume price correlates with quality here. Nearly everything on this list is free to join, and The Elite Trainer Academy is the one paid exception precisely because it serves a different audience, working trainers, not because paid content is inherently better than free content in this niche.

Look at who's actually running the community and what their stated expertise is. A pharmacist-led GLP-1 hub, coach-led bodybuilding crew, or podcast-affiliated trainer community are each making a specific credibility claim you can verify, which matters more in health and fitness than in almost any other Skool category.

Member count is a starting point, not a verdict. A smaller, specific community like Leon Edwards Crew at 5,010 members can be more useful for someone with a bodybuilding goal than a much larger general group, if the coaching inside is more relevant to what you're actually trying to do.

Watch for who the community is actually built for. The Elite Trainer Academy is the one paid community on this list precisely because it's training coaches, not clients, a completely different value proposition than a free group like OSX that's built for the person trying to lose fat or build muscle themselves.
